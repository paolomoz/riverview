We strive to provide compassionate, effective and efficient treatment to help individuals achieve their goals.

We are dedicated to providing compassionate, high-quality care for you and your family through a team of skilled healthcare providers who prioritize your well-being. Our providers take the time to listen to your concerns, understand your unique health needs, and deliver personalized care in a supportive, comfortable environment.

Lisa strives to partner with her patients and develop a personalized care plan that meets their unique needs. Health and wellness have always been a big part of Lisa’s life. She enjoys staying active and has competed in three NCAA Division I swimming championships in her lifetime. Lisa is passionate about collaborating with her patients to help them pursue a healthy lifestyle as well, and she enjoys helping others identify ways to integrate activities that encourage movement into their lives.

Originally from a northwest suburb of Chicago, Bailey pursued her undergraduate degree and physician assistant education at Butler University. Prior to becoming a physician assistant, she worked in emergency medicine for almost five years.

As a nurse practitioner, Kiersten strives to treat each patient she meets as a whole person. Kiersten prioritizes listening carefully during patient visits and she works to help them reach their health and wellness goals.

Family nurse practitioner Lisa Mickelson is dedicated to helping patients of all ages with a wide variety of issues and concerns. Her approach to care is to prioritize patient education, ensuring her patients understand their care plan and feel empowered on their journey back to better health. Originally from Chicago, Mickelson has had the opportunity to travel the world and live abroad in Germany and China. Stacia Frazee, NP is a board-certified family medicine nurse practitioner who excels at connecting with patients on a personal level, striving to treat each individual as a whole person. She holds a Master of Science in Nursing from the University of Indianapolis and is certified by the American Academy of Nurse Practitioners. Stacia’s personal practice philosophy revolves around empathy. She believes that each person has their own experiences, abilities, strengths and weaknesses – all of which can impact their health and wellbeing. Through empathetic care, she provides positive health outcomes so her patients can thrive through every stage of life.

As a family nurse practitioner, Whitney has the opportunity to care for people of all ages. She loves to meet new people and get to know them. Dr. Sharpe, a board-certified family physician, received his medical degree from Indiana University. Dr. Sharpe’s specialty is Family Medicine, and his clinical interests include disease prevention, injury care and young-adult care.

Dr. Kimberly Moran has a passion for helping others.
