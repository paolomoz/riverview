We’re deeply grateful you chose Riverview Health for your healthcare needs.

If a member of our team went the extra mile for you, perhaps offering comfort, sharing a laugh or listening intently to your concerns, we invite you to recognize them today.

The Riverview Health Champions of Care program provides an avenue for you to honor your Riverview Health team member(s) who positively impacted your care, while also benefiting programs and services at our not-for-profit hospital.

As a nonprofit healthcare provider, our goal is to champion health and wellness for all, and your gift helps us achieve this mission.

By contributing to the Riverview Health Foundation, no matter the size of your gift, you not only make a tax-deductible donation but also celebrate the healthcare champions who touched your life. Riverview Health caregivers will be honored with a unique symbol of recognition, celebrating their influence and expressing your gratitude. Honor a Riverview Health provider or staff member today. With your donation, you will help ensure important patient programs and services continue to be made available and that our champions are recognized and thanked.

DAISY Award: Recognize a Nurse

The DAISY Award was created to honor and recognize nurses for the outstanding effort they make to provide extraordinary care to patients every day. Riverview Health is proud to be a partner of the DAISY Award program as we recognize our nurses for their clinical skills, compassion and adherence to our core qualities:

• Excellent service• Excellent attitude• Excellent teamwork• Excellent safety

Nurse administrators, peers, physicians, patients and families can nominate Riverview Health nurses for the DAISY Award by completing the form. Nominations received by the 15th of each month will be considered for the DAISY Award the following month.

Golden Oak Award: Recognize a Non-nurse Team Member

The Golden Oak award recognizes any non-nurse Riverview Health team member. Nominees are those who went above and beyond in their job duties to provide the best experience possible for our patients and guests. These employees hold themselves accountable to the highest standards and constantly search for ways to improve through learning, process improvement and job performance.
