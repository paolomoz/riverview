Does being around animals make your eyes red and itchy? Do you have a sore throat and runny nose at certain times of the year? Whether you’re experiencing seasonal allergies or have a reaction to specific foods, allergies can interrupt your everyday life with unfortunate symptoms.

Normally, allergens are harmless, but when you have allergies, your body thinks they’re harmful and attacks them with antibodies. Antibodies are attached to cells called mast cells. Allergens stick to the antibodies, making the mast cells release histamine and other chemicals, and causing an allergic reaction.

Allergy Symptoms

If you have allergies, you may experience any of the following symptoms:

  • Coughing
  • Ear congestion
  • Fever
  • Headache
  • Hives
  • Itchy nose, eyes, ears and throat
  • Rash
  • Runny nose
  • Sinus pain and swelling
  • Sneezing
  • Stuffy nose
  • Swelling
  • Throat drainage
  • Watery, red eyes

Reliable Allergy Treatment in Noblesville, IN

When you visit a Riverview Health primary care physician, we may order an allergy test to determine the cause of your allergies and refer you to an allergy specialist if needed.

Types of allergy tests include:

  • Blood tests: An allergen is added to a blood sample, and if you have a reaction, it shows sensitivity to the allergen.
  • Skin tests: A small amount of each allergen is placed on the skin, then the sites are examined for a reaction like redness, swelling or itching.

Once we diagnose your allergies, we’ll work with you to find an effective treatment option for your unique needs. Common allergy treatments we provide include immunotherapy, antihistamines, steroids, injections, epinephrine, nasal sprays and more. We also recommend avoiding your allergens to prevent a reaction.
