At Riverview Health, we strive to provide exceptional care for each patient who walks through our doors. Our staff works very hard to support patients—not only in their physical health, but also their emotional and spiritual health.

Many of our patients and their families choose to show their gratitude for the care they’ve received by donating to Riverview Health Foundation. With a donation to Riverview Health Foundation, you’re continuing to support the health and wellness of our community. Gifts made to Riverview Health Foundation are used to support and expand many Riverview Health service areas, including cardiology, oncology, emergency medicine and surgery.

Meet Tom and Soni Sheehan

Tom and Soni Sheehan moved to Noblesville in 1984, at a time when Riverview Health was growing. Through the years, their family watched as Riverview Health continued to grow exponentially with its physicians, services, programs and advanced medical technologies. Riverview Health has now become a major provider for healthcare throughout central Indiana.

“As the hospital improved, so did my business. We were looking for ways to give back, and Riverview Health became a prominent cause for us because we wanted to serve our community. We choose Riverview Health because of the excellent care provided with a personal touch and family like environment. You’re treated like an individual. I don’t think I could go anywhere else and feel as comfortable.”-Tom Sheehan

Tom and Soni choose to support Riverview Health Foundation in order to serve their community.
