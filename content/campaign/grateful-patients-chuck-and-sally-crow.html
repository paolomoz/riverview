Grateful Patients: Chuck and Sally Crow

At Riverview Health, we do our best to provide exceptional care for each patient who walks through our doors. Our staff works very hard to support patients—not only in their physical health, but their emotional and spiritual health as well.

"Riverview Health has been an intimate part of my health for as long as I can remember. My favorite part of Riverview Health is the people. I know these people, and I feel confident that they will do the best they can to take care of me."

Many of our patients and their families choose to show their gratitude for the care they have received by donating to Riverview Health Foundation. With a donation to Riverview Health Foundation, you are continuing to support the health and wellness of our community. Gifts made to Riverview Health Foundation are used to support and expand many Riverview Health service areas, including cardiology, oncology, women's health, emergency medicine and surgery.

Chuck and Sally Share Their Story

Chuck and Sally Crow have long-standing roots at Riverview Health. The Crows have been giving to Riverview Health since 1976, when the Riverview Health Foundation was first chartered. Sally’s roots go even deeper—her father was a physician at Riverview Health, and her brother was born at the hospital.

Sally is passionate about the Women of Vision Giving Club, while Chuck has been a significant supporter of the Heartfelt Thanks Golf Tournament and Brick Layer Recognition Event through his business, Community Bank.

Although Chuck and Sally spend part of the year in Florida, they agree they’d come back to Riverview Health for any health concerns.
