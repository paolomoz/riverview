Foundation - Frequently Asked Questions

How can I donate to Riverview Health Foundation? You can donate online through our secure donation website, or by calling our Foundation office-317.776.7317.

Is my donation tax-deductible? Yes. Riverview Health Foundation is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization, and all donations are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law. After your donation, you will receive confirmation and tax receipt for your records.

Can I donate in honor or memory of someone? Absolutely. You can dedicate your gift in honor or memory of a loved one. We will send a notification of your gift to the person or family you designated.

What types of donations do you accept? We welcome one-time gifts, monthly recurring donations, corporate gifts, and donations for our fundraising events. Contact us to learn more about planned and non-cash gifts.

How is my donation used? Your gift supports priority needs at Riverview Health, including medical equipment, patient support services, women of vision giving club, hospital rehab unit developments etc . You can also designate your donation to a specific department or initiative.

Can I choose where my donation goes? Yes, you can direct your gift to a specific program, department, or fund that is meaningful to you. Simply indicate your preference when making your donation.

Is it safe to donate online? Yes. Our online donation system uses industry-standard encryption and security protocols to protect your personal and financial information.

Will I receive a receipt? Yes. All donors receive a receipt via email or mail, which you can use for tax purposes.

How can I become a monthly donor? When you donate online, simply select the option for “monthly giving.” You can change or cancel your recurring gift at any time.

Who can I contact with questions about giving? Please reach out to the Riverview Health Foundation office below. We are more than happy to assist you!
