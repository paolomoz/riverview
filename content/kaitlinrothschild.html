Kaitlin Rothschild, PA

Kaitlin Rothschild, PA, is a physician assistant specializing in gastroenterology who enjoys building genuine connections with patients while providing thoughtful, long-term care. She is committed to helping patients understand their condition and feel confident in their care plan.

Kaitlin strives to create a safe and comfortable environment at every visit. She is attentive, thorough, and places a strong emphasis on patient education and prevention. Outside of work, she enjoys spending time with her husband, son, and their German Shepherd, Dempsey, as well as traveling and horseback riding.
