Employer Services

Your organization depends on a healthy, productive workforce that’s sustainable. To ensure your workforce stays healthy and well, it’s vital to promote a culture of health and wellness with a strong emphasis on disease prevention. The first step is to provide health and wellness programming so employees and their dependents feel empowered to take charge of their own health.

Chronic diseases such as depression and high blood pressure (hypertension) can lead to a decline in the overall health of employees at the workplace, which contributes to an increase in health-related expenses for employers and employees. These conditions can also lead to lower productivity or days missed from work.

Riverview Health, a 5-star AchieveWell awardee by the Wellness Council of Indiana, offers many services to help employers develop and implement a customized wellness program. Wellness programs help employees engage and change short- and long-term behaviors and gain better overall health. Our programs and services target and identify health risks and offer recommendations for a successful health management and engagement strategy.
