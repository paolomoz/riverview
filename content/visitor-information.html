Visitor Information

We recognize the vital role that family and friends play in the healing and comfort of our patients. We are committed to providing a safe, welcoming, and respectful environment for all visitors.

We recognize the vital role that family and friends play in the healing and comfort of our patients. We are committed to providing a safe, welcoming, and respectful environment for all visitors.

Visiting hours are limited to 8 a.m. to 8 p.m. every day. Visitors should not visit if they are ill or are experiencing COVID like symptoms.

Westfield Hospital visitors should use the main entrance.

Donations, volunteers, and partnerships allow Riverview Health to provide community-minded and patient-centered care to all, regardless of someone’ s ability to pay.
