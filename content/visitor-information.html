Code of conduct

Riverview Health is committed to providing high-quality care to our patients and communities in a safe and respectful environment that supports health and healing.

To ensure our hospitals and care locations are safe and caring, we ask that patients and visitors follow the example of Riverview Health providers and employees by adhering to our Patient and Visitor Code of Conduct.

Patient messages & prayer requests

If you can't visit a Riverview patient in person, we encourage you to send a message through our online form. A Riverview volunteer will hand-deliver it between 9 a.m. and 4 p.m., Monday to Friday. Messages submitted after 4 p.m., or on weekends and holidays, will be delivered the next business day.

Flu & Covid guidelines

At Riverview Health, the safety of our patients, staff, employees and visitors are our top priority. We follow strict guidelines and restrictions for visitors with flu or covid like symptoms to reduce the transmission.
