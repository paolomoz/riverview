Foundation50

Celebrating 50 years of service, community growth, and commitment to our mission. This milestone honors our legacy and looks ahead to a future of continued excellence.

Donate Now

As we celebrate 50 years of service, I am deeply grateful for the countless individuals, partners, and supporters who have shaped this Foundation’s journey. What began as a shared belief in making a meaningful difference has grown into five decades of impact, compassion, and community.

Since our founding in 1976, we have raised almost $19 million to support innovative projects that enhance patient care - most recently, the Mindrebo Family Acute Inpatient Rehabilitation Center as well as the Courtney Cox Cole Infusion Center in Noblesville.

Our mission remains as strong today as it was at the beginning—and it’s your generosity and dedication that continues to move us forward. Thank you for standing with us, believing in our work, and helping us create a legacy that will inspire the next 50 years and beyond.

Megan G. Wiles

Vice President, Foundation and Community Affairs

For fifty years, the true strength of Hamilton County has been its people — neighbors who show up, families who give what they can, and a community that believes in caring for one another. Riverview Health Foundation has been the bridge connecting that generosity to real change, helping every gift become a healthier child, a comforted parent, a life renewed.

Together, we have strengthened our hospitals. Together, we have supported our caregivers. Together, we have built a legacy of compassion that will outlast us all.

Here’s to a community that never stops giving — and to the healthier tomorrows we will continue creating, side by side.

Whether supporting the Women of Vision Giving Club, Champions of Care program, Courtney Cox Cole Infusion Center, Help for Healing Fund (a patient financial assistance fund) or the Area of Greatest Need, every dollar invested in Riverview Health Foundation goes to fund programs and projects that benefit the patients and staff of Riverview Health.
