Gordon Brick Layer Club

The Gordon Brick Layer Club includes members from the community who have made a contribution of $1,000 to $9,999 to Riverview Health Foundation.

Gordon Brick Layer Club members are invited to special events and Riverview Health seminars. Their names are also displayed on the recognition wall in the Riverview Health Atrium.

To join our list of members, contact Riverview Health Foundation at 317.776.7317 or mwiles@riverview.org.
