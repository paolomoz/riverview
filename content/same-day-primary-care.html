From routine vaccines and physicals to more complex health needs, Riverview Health delivers compassionate, high‑quality care at every visit.

We treat the following conditions for same-day appointments:

  • Cold, flu, fever, COVID‑like symptoms
  • Infections (sinus, ear, UTI, skin)
  • Allergies & asthma
  • Stomach issues
  • Minor injuries & pain
  • Chronic condition check‑ins (diabetes, blood pressure, thyroid)
  • If you’re unsure, we can help you decide. Call to book an appointment: 317.565.0565.

Why Choose Us

  • Same‑Day access and care when you need it the most
  • Primary care, not Urgent Care
  • We focus on your overall health and on-going care
  • Board‑certified physicians

Same‑day primary care is not intended for emergencies. In case of emergency, dial 911 or seek care at the nearest emergency or urgent care facility.
