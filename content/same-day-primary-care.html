From routine vaccines and physicals to more complex health needs, Riverview Health delivers compassionate, high‑quality care at every visit.

Do I really get seen the same day?

Yes. Appointments are reserved daily for same day visits. Availability may vary by location and provider, booking early is encouraged.

Is this urgent care?

No. This is primary care, which means your visit focuses on both immediate concerns and your overall, ongoing health.

What if my condition is more serious that the general concers?

If your provider determines you need a higher level of care, they will help coordinate next steps, including referrals or emergency care if needed.

Can I do a virtual visit instead of coming in?

Yes. Virtual visits are available for many concerns when clinically appropriate.

Do I need to be an existing patient?

No. New and returning patients are welcome to book same day appointments.

What should I bring to my appointment?

Please bring a photo ID, your insurance card, a list of current medications, and any relevant medical information.

Can you prescribe medication or order labs?

Yes. Providers can prescribe medications, order labs, and provide referrals when medically appropriate.

Do you accept my insurance?

We accept most major insurance plans. Talk to our scheduling assistants about insurance coverage.
