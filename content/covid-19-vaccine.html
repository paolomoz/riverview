COVID-19 Vaccine

We're proud that we were among the first vaccination sites to open up in the state. Since we began vaccinating in December 2020, we've had several employees step up to offer their time––away from their usual duties––to help our vaccine clinic run smoothly. As more vaccination sites open up in the state, we have closed our vaccine clinic.

We are following CDC guidance on vaccinations and booster shots. Learn more here >>

At this time, Riverview Health is not providing third dose/booster vaccinations. Please visit www.ourshot.in.gov to find a location if you meet the CDC criteria and your healthcare provider recommends that you receive the third dose.

To schedule an appointment elsewhere, please either visit ourshot.in.gov or call 2-1-1. Appointments are required.

Please do not call Riverview Health hospitals or practices to schedule a COVID-19 vaccination appointment, as our phone operators and staff are unable to schedule for you. Instead, you will be directed to call the state's hotline at 2-1-1 or visit ourshot.in.gov.

There should be no out of pocket cost to anyone for a COVID-19 vaccination.

The state of Indiana will bill your insurance company for the administration of your COVID-19 vaccine, but you should not have a copay or coinsurance due. If you receive information that you have a copay or coinsurance due, please contact your insurance company. If they don’t cancel the bill, contact the HHS Office of Inspector General Hotline at 1.800.HHS.TIPS or visit https://TIPS.HHS.GOV to file a complaint.

For more information about the vaccines and eligibility, please visit theIndiana State Department of Health website >>

Scroll down to the Indiana Vaccination Portal and click on it. Then click the gold button that says “Log In to Application”

Follow the instructions provided to set up an account or log in if you already have an account.

After entering your name, date of birth, gender and zip code, your vaccine results will be displayed. From this summary screen, choose the “DOWNLOAD VACCINATION RESULTS” link. The document generated by this link will contain all necessary details of the COVID19 vaccination record, including: Product received Date(s) received Lot number The facility where vaccine was administered

You may also click the “DOWNLOAD VACCINE CERTIFICATE” link, but that document is less specific and does not appear to contain the information required by most interested parties (cruise ships, future employers, etc.).
