Featured Classes

Safe Sitter Classes

Whether your daughter or son is just starting to babysit or is being trusted to care for siblings, this class will help her or him learn necessary skills to be an effective caregiver. This class is only for students in grades 5-8.

Preparation for Childbirth

In this online program, you'll learn everything you need to know to prepare for labor, delivery and beyond. This class is geared toward patients who will be delivering at our Noblesville, Indiana location.

Breastfeeding Class

This self-paced online program provides breastfeeding tips and techniques for new moms and those needing a refresher. We'll also discuss how to best meet your infant’s nutritional needs.
