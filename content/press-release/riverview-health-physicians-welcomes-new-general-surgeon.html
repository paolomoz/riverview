Riverview Health Physicians welcomes new general surgeon

September 5, 2023

Noblesville—Riverview Health Physicians is pleased to welcome Carly Thoma-Perry, DO to Hamilton Surgical Associates.

As a general surgeon, Dr. Thoma-Perry’s clinical interests include treating breast and colon cancer patients, minimally invasive surgeries and gastrointestinal surgeries. From emergency surgeries to elective, outpatient procedures, she is proud to stand by her patients and help them through what can be their most challenging times. She is committed to providing patients with the most up to date treatment options.

Dr. Thoma-Perry graduated from Marian University College of Osteopathic Medicine. She enjoys cooking, hiking, traveling and reading historical nonfiction.

To schedule an appointment, please call 317.776.8748.
