Riverview Health Physicians welcomes new OB/GYN physician

August 10, 2021

Riverview Health is pleased to welcome Holly Storm, DO, to Riverview Health Physicians OB/GYN.

Dr. Storm believes the best patient care for women is achieved when they are empowered and can advocate for themselves. Dr. Storm strives to build a genuine relationship with each of her patients through education, collaboration and listening to their needs. Dr. Storm treats every patient as an individual, and it is her goal for them to feel confident in their plan of care.

Dr. Storm grew up in the small town of Bristol, Indiana. She completed two undergraduate degrees before attending medical school at Marian University College of Osteopathic Medicine. When Dr. Storm is not working, she enjoys spending time with her husband and son. She also loves to travel.

To schedule an appointment with Dr. Storm, call 317.770.6085.
