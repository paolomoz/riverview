Riverview Health Physicians welcomes new family medicine physician

August 28, 2023

Westfield—Riverview Health is pleased to welcome Bryce Buente, DO to Westfield Medical.

As a family medicine physician, Dr. Buente provides care to patients of all ages. He enjoys helping patients with the management of chronic medical conditions including diabetes, high blood pressure and obesity. He is trained in different procedures including joint injections, skin biopsies and more.

Dr. Buente graduated from Marian University College of Osteopathic Medicine. He enjoys powerlifting, playing chess and spending time with his family.

To schedule an appointment with Dr. Buente, call 317.867.5263.
