Riverview Health to Host Seminar on Treating Shoulder & Knee Pain

April 21, 2022

Riverview Health will host a treatment of shoulder and knee pain seminar from 6-7 p.m. on Wednesday, May 11.

Join board-certified and fellowship-trained physician, Scott Smith, MD, to learn about the treatments available for shoulder and knee pain. Dr. Smith will highlight both surgical and non-surgical methods for addressing pain, as well as his specialization in shoulder and knee replacement surgery.

The seminar will take place at Riverview Health, 395 Westfield Rd., Noblesville, IN 46060 in the Krieg DeVault Conference Room, located in the lower level of the Women’s Pavilion. The program is free, but registration is required. Attendees are required to wear masks. Register at riverview.org/classes or call 317.776.7999.
