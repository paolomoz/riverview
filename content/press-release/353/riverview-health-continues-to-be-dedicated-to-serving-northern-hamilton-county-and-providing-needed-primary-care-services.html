Riverview Health continues to be dedicated to serving northern Hamilton County and providing needed primary care services

December 4, 2023

Cicero Family Care, a primary care practice of Riverview Health, opened the doors to its new office space on Monday, Nov. 20. Now located at 1160 S. Peru Street, the practice has expanded its patient capacity with nine exam rooms and a larger waiting room.

Riverview Health is hosting an open house and ribbon cutting on Thursday, Dec. 7, from 3-6:00 p.m. The OneZone Chamber of Commerce will join us for a ribbon cutting ceremony at 4:00 p.m. We hope you will join us for this exciting event!

Cicero Family Care has been a part of the northern Hamilton County community for decades. The practice was opened by Dr. Charles Harris on the north side of downtown Cicero. The office space was converted from Dr. Harris’ home at the time and over the years has grown to respond to the community’s healthcare needs.

When Dr. Harris retired in 2019, Dr. Jeremy Fisk, longtime physician with Riverview Health, stepped in to ensure care was continued for the Cicero community. Along with David Henry, physician assistant, the team at Cicero provides care for all ages. We are also excited to share that Dr. Megan Eaton will be joining the practice in January 2024.

“This new location for Cicero Family Care allows us to expand our services and be better able to provide timely, high-quality care in the community of Cicero,” said Dr. Jeremy Fisk. “As the needs of the community change over time, we will have the flexibility to respond. I am looking forward to the opportunities that having this location will provide to continue to serve the people of Cicero and surrounding areas with exceptional care.”

For more information, visit riverview.org. To schedule an appointment with Cicero Family Care, call 317.984.9311.
