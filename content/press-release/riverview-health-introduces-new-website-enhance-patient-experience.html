Riverview Health Introduces New Website to Enhance Patient Experience

June 30, 2025

NOBLESVILLE, IN — Riverview Health is proud to announce the official launch of its newly designed website, offering a more intuitive, user-friendly experience for patients, jobseekers and visitors. 

The new site features streamlined design and updated content, making it easier than ever to find information, access services and connect with providers. Riverview Health proudly offers same-day appointments for Primary Care and Orthopedic services, ensuring you get the prompt care you need when you need it most. With improved scheduling functionality, users can book appointments by provider, location or through MyChart. 

The website includes new and improved job search abilities designed to attract top talent, offering better navigation and more information about open roles and hiring events. Furthermore, advanced Search Engine Optimization tools help improve visibility across search platforms, making it simpler for users to connect with Riverview Health. 

“We are very excited to unveil this new website as it now appropriately reflects our exceptional commitment to improving and preserving the health and well-being of those we serve,” said Dave Hyatt, President and CEO of Riverview Health. “This new platform is designed with our community in mind and makes connecting with compassionate, patient-centered care easier than ever.” 

To explore the new website, visit riverview.org.

About Riverview Health 

Riverview Health is an independent, community-based health system offering comprehensive healthcare services throughout Hamilton County. The system’s network includes a 156-bed hospital in Noblesville, a 16-bed hospital in Westfield, three additional combined ER and urgent care facilities and 10 primary care locations. With expertise in more than 25 specialties, Riverview Health also provides a full range of inpatient and outpatient services and is consistently recognized for clinical and service excellence. Most recently, Riverview Health was certified as a Great Place to Work® and received the Culture of Good Community Impact Award for 2025.
