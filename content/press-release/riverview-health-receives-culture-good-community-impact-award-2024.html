Riverview Health Receives the Culture of Good Community Impact Award 2024

August 15, 2024

Riverview Health is proud to announce that we are a Culture of Good Community Impact Award 2024 winner! This award celebrates companies for their outstanding commitment to community service and using their business as a force for positive change in the world.​ We are honored to be one of 15 companies to be recognized across the entire country in the inaugural year of this award!

Receiving Culture of Good’s Community Impact Award reflects Riverview Health's collective efforts as a team to improve and preserve the health and well-being of those we serve. It's a testament to the dedication and passion of our employees, who consistently strive to make a difference in the lives of others and embody our values in everything they do. ​ To further Riverview Health's community impact, consider donating to the Riverview Health Foundation at donate.riverview.org.
