Riverview Health Earns 2025 Great Place To Work Certification

January 20, 2025

NOBLESVILLE, IN — Riverview Health is proud to be Certified™ by Great Place To Work® for 2025. The prestigious award is based entirely on what current employees say about their experience working at Riverview Health. This year, 79% of employee respondents said it’s a Great Place To Work – 22% higher than the average U.S. company.

“We are honored and excited to be Certified by Great Place To Work,” said Riverview Health President and CEO Dave Hyatt. “Our vision is to become the best place to work, practice medicine and receive care in the communities we serve. This recognition demonstrates our significant progress toward this goal, thanks to the sustained commitment and diligence of all members of the Riverview Health team.”

Great Place To Work® is the global authority on workplace culture, employee experience, and the leadership behaviors proven to deliver market-leading revenue, employee retention and increased innovation.

"Great Place To Work Certification is a highly coveted achievement that requires consistent and intentional dedication to the overall employee experience," says Sarah Lewis-Kulin, the Vice President of Global Recognition at Great Place To Work. She emphasizes that Certification is the sole official recognition earned by the real-time feedback of employees regarding their company culture. “By successfully earning this recognition, it is evident that Riverview Health stands out as one of the top companies to work for, providing a great workplace environment for its employees."

Riverview Health is proud to foster an environment that empowers and supports its employees at every stage of their careers. By prioritizing professional development, cultivating a collaborative workplace culture, and driving opportunities for career advancement, Riverview Health underscores its dedication to both individual and organizational growth. With a focus on continuous learning and leadership development, Riverview Health ensures its team members have the tools and resources they need to thrive in their roles and prepare for future leadership opportunities within the organization.

“Our people are our top priority, and the Great Place To Work Certification is a testament to the dedication and passion of our employees, who consistently strive to make a difference in the lives of others,” said Chief Ambulatory Officer Nikki Swiney. “We thank every Riverview Health employee for all they do to embody our values of Compassion, Belonging and Excellence and earn this incredible recognition.”

According to Great Place To Work research, job seekers are 4.5 times more likely to find a great boss at a Certified great workplace. Additionally, employees at Certified workplaces are 93% more likely to look forward to coming to work and are twice as likely to be paid fairly.

Riverview Health is currently hiring across a variety of clinical and non-clinical positions and locations. With a commitment to employee growth and well-being, Riverview Health is the perfect place to build your career while making a meaningful impact in healthcare. Visit our careers page for more information: https://riverview.org/careers/

About Riverview Health

Riverview Health is an independent community-based health system comprised of a full-service, 156-bed hospital in Noblesville, a 16-bed hospital in Westfield, as well as freestanding combined ER and urgent care facilities. Also included are 14 distinct primary, immediate, and specialty care facilities across Hamilton County. Riverview Health provides comprehensive inpatient and outpatient services in more than 35 healthcare specialties and has been frequently recognized for its clinical and service excellence.

About Great Place to Work Certification™

Great Place To Work® Certification™ is the most definitive “employer-of-choice” recognition that companies aspire to achieve. It is the only recognition based entirely on what employees report about their workplace experience – specifically, how consistently they experience a high-trust workplace. Great Place to Work Certification is recognized worldwide by employees and employers alike and is the global benchmark for identifying and recognizing outstanding employee experience. Every year, more than 10,000 companies across 60 countries apply to get Great Place To Work-Certified.

About Great Place To Work®

As the global authority on workplace culture, Great Place To Work® brings 30 years of groundbreaking research and data to help every place become a great place to work for all. Their proprietary platform and For All™ Model helps companies evaluate the experience of every employee, with exemplary workplaces becoming Great Place To Work Certified™ or receiving recognition on a coveted Best Workplaces™ List.
