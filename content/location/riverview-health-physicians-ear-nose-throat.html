About this location

Otolaryngology, also known as ENT (ear, nose, and throat), is a medical specialty focused on treating conditions affecting the ears, nose, and throat. Otolaryngologists are trained in both medicine and surgery, and they can perform head and neck surgeries.

Riverview Health’s board-certified Ear, Nose, and Throat (ENT) physicians are dedicated to treating patients with high quality care and cutting-edge technologies. Our team of highly skilled specialists offers a wide range of services to address various ear, nose, and throat conditions for both adults and children.

We focus on treating a wide range of allergy & sinus issues with comprehensive management of allergies and sinusitis including allergy testing, immuno-therapy, and advanced sinus surgery options like balloon sinuplasty and endoscopic sinus surgery.

Utilizing advanced diagnostic tools and minimally invasive surgical techniques, we provide treatment for head, neck and throat conditions including thyroid disorders, voice and swallowing issues, and head and neck cancers.

For hearing and balance issues, we offer comprehensive audiological services including hearing aids, cochlear implants, and balance disorder treatments. We also provide diagnostic evaluations and personalized treatment plans to manage hearing loss and balance issues.

In addition, we offer evaluation and treatment of various voice disorders such as hoarseness, vocal cord nodules, and paralysis. We take a multidisciplinary approach involving laryngologists and speech pathologists to provide tailored treatment plans.
