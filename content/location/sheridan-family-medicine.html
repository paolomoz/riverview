About this location

At Sheridan Family Medicine, you’ll find comprehensive medical care for the whole family in a convenient location.

From routine physicals to the diagnosis and treatment of acute and chronic illnesses, our physicians are here to care for your medical needs and provide you with peace-of-mind.

Our physicians are highly skilled in caring for individuals of all ages. We employ the latest technologies and treatments to keep you feeling your best. We work to establish long-standing physician-patient relationships and to serve as your partner in your overall health and wellness management.

We realize healthcare comes with many questions. At Sheridan Family Medicine, our physicians and staff are dedicated to providing thorough answers to your health questions, and will work to proactively address your health concerns.

For your convenience, Riverview Health Physicians patient registration forms are available below. When you make your appointment, the receptionist will let you know which set of forms to complete.

Please download, print and complete the applicable forms and bring the completed documents, your photo ID and your insurance card(s) with you to your appointment.
