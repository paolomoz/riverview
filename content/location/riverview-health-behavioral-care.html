About this location

The staff of Riverview Health Behavioral Care is dedicated to serving our community through life’s ups and downs. We provide psychological assessment and treatment to individuals struggling with anxiety, bipolar disorder, depression, insomnia, PTSD/trauma, stress-related disorders, OCD, tobacco use and weight management.

Our providers strive to provide compassionate, effective and efficient treatment to help individuals achieve their goals. We partner with you in your behavioral health journey by providing a compassionate, healing environment. We’ll listen to what has been bothering you and help you identify any conditions and develop a care plan.
