About this location

The staff of Hazel Dell Family Care in Carmel is dedicated to providing the highest quality medical care in a welcoming environment. Our physician is trained to diagnose and treat you, whether your visit is for a routine physical or a more pressing health concern.

You can rest assured at Hazel Dell Family Care, you’ll receive the comprehensive care you deserve and the answers you need.

Our staff is committed to treating all patients in a caring and compassionate manner. A strong physician-patient relationship is our priority. We work with every patient to guide participation in individual health and wellness management.

For your convenience, Riverview Health Physicians patient registration forms are available below. When you make your appointment, the receptionist will let you know which set of forms to complete.

Please download, print and complete the applicable forms and bring the completed documents, your photo ID and your insurance card(s) with you to your appointment.
