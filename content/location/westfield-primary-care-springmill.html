About this location

The providers and staff at Westfield Primary Care Spring Mill are dedicated to delivering high-quality primary care in a compassionate, welcoming environment. Our care team provides comprehensive medical services for patients of all ages, supporting the health and wellbeing of your entire family.

We focus on building strong, trusted relationships with our patients so we can better understand your needs and help you achieve your health goals. Whether you are visiting us for preventive care, treatment of an acute illness, or management of a chronic condition, our team is here to provide attentive, personalized care.

For your convenience, Riverview Health Physicians patient registration forms are available below. When you make your appointment, the receptionist will let you know which set of forms to complete.

Please download, print and complete the applicable forms and bring the completed documents, your photo ID and your insurance card(s) with you to your appointment.
