About this location

Injury or illness can contribute to a number of debilitating issues affecting the foot and ankle. As the foundation of the body, our highly trained specialist at Riverview Health understands how important it is to keep your lower extremities healthy and pain-free—not only to keep you mobile, but to help you enjoy life to the fullest.

Our team is ready to treat a variety of conditions of the foot and ankle through surgical or non-surgical management. During your visit, our specialist will listen carefully to your concerns and work collaboratively with you to determine the most effective treatment plan for your unique needs.

If you require surgical intervention, our dedicated team will guide you every step of the way. Our specialist will provide ample education regarding the pre- and post-operative process, and also connect you with rehabilitation providers as appropriate.
