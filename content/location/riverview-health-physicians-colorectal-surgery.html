About this location

The board-certified surgeons at Hamilton Surgical Associates in Noblesville and Westfield perform advanced surgical procedures.

Our primary goal is to provide excellent care to patients who need surgery for abdominal, rectal, breast and chest diseases and issues. Commonly performed procedures include breast surgery, abdominal hernia repair, gallbladder removal, surgery of the colon, placement of vascular access devices, vasectomies and surgery of the chest.

Routine surgical procedures are performed in the office, and more complex surgeries are performed in the Riverview Health hospitals in Noblesville or Westfield.

We recognize that preparing for a surgical procedure comes with a lot of questions. Our team is here to guide you through the process—helping you understand what to expect before, during and after your surgical procedure.

For your convenience, Riverview Health Physicians patient registration forms are available below. When you make your appointment, the receptionist will let you know which set of forms to complete.

Please download, print and complete the applicable forms and bring the completed documents, your photo ID and your insurance card(s) with you to your appointment.
