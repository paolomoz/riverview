About this location

With a history of more than 100 years in Hamilton County, Riverview Health has brought a tradition of high-quality, compassionate healthcare to the city of Westfield. Riverview Health Westfield Hospital opened in 2018 and offers a variety of services to the people of Westfield and surrounding communities.

The hospital holds the area’s first combined emergency and urgent care center. When you’re injured, you may not know what level of care you need. Having urgent care and emergency services together with one convenient access point means you don’t have to make that decision on your own. At Riverview Health Westfield Hospital, you come to one place and will be guided to the level of care you need, and you will be billed for the level of care you receive.
