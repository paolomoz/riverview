About this location

Riverview Health is comprised of hospitals in Noblesville and Westfield and 25 primary, immediate and specialty care facilities located throughout Hamilton County.

The Noblesville location is a full-service, 156-bed hospital situated along the banks of the White River, in the heart of Noblesville. As a non-profit, county-owned health system, we proudly serve the members of our community—regardless of their situation or condition.

We’ve been a part of the healthcare landscape in Noblesville for more than 100 years—and we’re still going strong. Today, we provide a full spectrum of healthcare services, including heart and vascular, cancer, orthopedic, surgical, emergency, and women’s care, among others.

Our main hospital in Noblesville, which is accredited by the Healthcare Facilities Accreditation Program (HFAP), is designed with you in mind. From our conveniently located outpatient service area to our maternity center with spa-like amenities, we’re always working to provide the best experience for our patients and guests. No detail is too small at Riverview Health—and we work hard to ensure you receive the comfort and attention you deserve.

In addition, many of our primary care and specialty physicians and staff have been recognized for their clinical and service excellence. While we’re proud of our achievements, we recognize the need to continually enhance our technologies, services and programs to better serve you and your family.

At our hospitals, you and your loved ones can count on receiving the comprehensive, expert care they’ve come to expect—provided by experts who are skilled at delivering care in more than 35 medical specialties.
