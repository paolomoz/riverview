About this location

The physicians and staff of Prairie Lakes Family Medicine are committed to providing a full spectrum of primary healthcare services for you and your family.

Our physicians strive to serve as your partner in the management of your health. From routine physicals to managing acute and chronic conditions, we’re here to provide the comprehensive, thorough healthcare you deserve. We help treat your physical, mental and emotional needs in all stages of your life.

Healthcare can be complex. Our physicians take the time to address all of your medical concerns and to answer your questions. Whether your visit is for a routine physical exam or a pressing health concern, you can rest assured our physicians will treat you with expert care.

For your convenience, Riverview Health Physicians patient registration forms are available below. When you make your appointment, the receptionist will let you know which set of forms to complete.

Please download, print and complete the applicable forms and bring the completed documents, your photo ID and your insurance card(s) with you to your appointment.
