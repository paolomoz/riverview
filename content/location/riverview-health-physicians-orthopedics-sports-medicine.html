About this location

Located just north of Indianapolis, Riverview Health Physicians Orthopedics & Sports Medicine has four convenient locations in Carmel, Noblesville and Westfield. Our orthopedics and sports medicine team is here to provide comprehensive care to people of all ages, with a focus on total musculoskeletal health, including bones, joints, tendons and nerves.

Our physicians provide personalized care while diagnosing and treating orthopedic and sports medicine conditions effectively and efficiently. We know your ultimate goal is to get back to work, play and everything in between as quickly as possible, and we’re here to make that happen.
