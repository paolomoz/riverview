Patient Messages & Prayer Requests

If you’re unable to visit a Riverview Health patient in person, you’re encouraged to send a message using the form on this page. Your message will be printed and hand-delivered by a Riverview Health volunteer.

Messages are delivered to patients between 9 a.m. and 4 p.m., Monday through Friday. Any messages submitted after 4 p.m., or on a holiday or weekend, will be delivered the following business day.

Please note patients can’t reply to messages sent via this form. Messages sent after a patient is discharged are securely discarded.

Our chaplain and chaplaincy volunteers are happy to pray for your loved one. Please use the form on this page to submit a prayer request.

Our chaplain may also be reached by phone at 317.776.7391 or dhansen@riverview.org.
