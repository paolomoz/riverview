Patient Messages and Prayer Requests

If you’re unable to visit a Riverview Health patient in person, you’re encouraged to send a message using the form on this page. Your message will be printed and hand-delivered by a Riverview Health volunteer.

Messages are delivered to patients between 9 a.m. and 4 p.m., Monday through Friday. Any messages submitted after 4 p.m., or on a holiday or weekend, will be delivered the following business day.

Please note patients can’t reply to messages sent via this form. Messages sent after a patient is discharged are securely discarded.

Chaplaincy Services & Submit a Prayer Request

Our chaplain and chaplaincy volunteers are happy to pray for your loved one. Please use the form on this page to submit a prayer request.

Our chaplain may also be reached by phone at 317.776.7391 or dhansen@riverview.org.

Learn more about chaplain services >>

How Message Delivery Works

Every message travels a short, personal route: you submit the form below, a Riverview Health volunteer prints it, and it is carried by hand to the patient's room. To help your note arrive quickly:

  • Choose the right request type — a patient message for room delivery or a prayer request for the chaplain.
  • Give the patient's full name, and include the room number whenever you know it so the volunteer can find the room without delay.
  • Send before 4 p.m. on a weekday if you'd like same-day delivery; later submissions arrive the next business day.

Prefer to visit in person? Check current visiting hours and entrance times on our visitor information page, or brighten a stay with flowers or a gift delivered to the room by Carolyn's Corner gift shop. Those seeking spiritual support beyond a single prayer request can read about chaplaincy services at Riverview Health.
