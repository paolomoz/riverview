At Riverview Health, you have access to a wide range of care and services across our health system. Riverview Health is structured to support patients with more complex or higher health needs through access to specialty physicians, advanced diagnostic services, and clinical resources, all working together to deliver high‑quality care.

  • Exceptional Care Led by Board-Certified PhysiciansOur team of highly skilled, board-certified physicians delivers expert, patient-centered care across a wide range of specialties, ensuring the highest standards of safety, quality, and outcomes. Learn more about our physicians
  • Enhanced Surgical Excellence with Three da Vinci 5 Robotic SystemsWith the addition of three state-of-the-art da Vinci 5 surgical robots, we are expanding our minimally invasive surgical capabilities—offering greater precision, faster recovery times, and improved patient experiences. Explore our surgery services
  • Elevating Breast Care with Leading Specialists and Advanced Treatment OptionsOur breast care program continues to grow with the addition of new, highly trained breast surgeons, enhancing access to compassionate, personalized care supported by the latest innovations in diagnosis and treatment. Learn more about advanced breast care
  • Transformative Healing in Our Newly Renovated Acute Rehabilitation UnitThe newly renovated Acute Rehabilitation Unit at our Noblesville hospital provides a modern, healing environment designed to support recovery, mobility, and independence with advanced therapies and expert care teams. Learn more about our Acute Rehabilitation Unit
  • State of the art Endoscopy Center Our state-of-the-art Endoscopy Center has been designed with comfort, safety, and innovation in mind. Whether you need a routine screening or a more specialized procedure, our team delivers accurate, efficient, and compassionate care. Learn more about our new Endoscopy center

We also recognize the importance of accessibility, family involvement, and patient experience in the healing process. Our Noblesville location and modern facilities are designed to support comfort, safety, and engagement for both patients and their families. At Riverview Health, our goal is to provide not just excellent clinical care, but a supportive environment that promotes recovery and long‑term health.
