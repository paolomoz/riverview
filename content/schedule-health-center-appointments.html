LiveHealth Online

Whether you have a cold, you’re feeling anxious or need help managing your medication, doctors and mental health professionals are right there, ready to help you feel your best. Using LiveHealth Online you can have a video visit with a board-certified doctor, psychiatrist or licensed therapist from your smartphone, tablet or computer from home or anywhere.

You’ve got access to affordable and convenient care Your Anthem plan includes benefits for video visits using LiveHealth Online, so you’ll just pay your share of the costs — usually $59 or less for medical doctor visits, and a 45-minute therapy or psychiatry session usually costs the same as an office mental health visit.
