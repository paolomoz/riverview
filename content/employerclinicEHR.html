Employer Clinic EHR Update

We are upgrading to a new electronic health record (EHR) system. This change will bring better care coordination, an improved digital experience with MyChart, and smoother workflows throughout your health center program. Launch: February 2026.

We are upgrading to a new electronic health record (EHR) system. This change will bring better care coordination, an improved digital experience with MyChart, and smoother workflows throughout your health center program. Launch: February 2026.

The new EHR system enables a more connected, seamless healthcare experience. With this transition, your employees will benefit from:

Improved Care CoordinationProviders will have faster, more comprehensive access to patient histories, medications, and previous visits.

Enhanced Scheduling & CommunicationThe new EHR System streamlines scheduling and allows for easier appointment management.

A Modern Digital ExperienceWith the new MyChart patient portal, employees can view test results, message their care team, manage appointments, and access health information on any device with a convenient and improved digital experience.

Track Health Care Journey and GoalsPatients can track immunizations, monitor upcoming preventive screenings, and receive helpful reminders, making it easier to stay informed and prioritize long‑term wellness.
