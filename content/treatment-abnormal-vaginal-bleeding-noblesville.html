Some possible causes of abnormal vaginal bleeding can include:

Vaginal cancer
Hyperthyroidism
Hypothyroidism
Polycystic ovarian syndrome
Birth control pills
Ectopic pregnancy
Chlamydia
Gonorrhea
Pelvic inflammatory disease (PID)
Uterine fibroids
Threatened miscarriage
