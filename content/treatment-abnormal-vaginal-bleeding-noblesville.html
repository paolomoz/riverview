Treatment for Abnormal Vaginal Bleeding in Noblesville, IN

Home

Abnormal vaginal bleeding is common in both young and adult women. Vaginal bleeding is considered abnormal if it meets any of the following conditions:

  • Occurs when you are not on your menstrual period
  • Occurs when you are younger than nine, pregnant or experiencing menopause
  • Affects your menstrual period (lighter or heavier than normal)

If you’re experiencing any of these conditions, it’s important to visit a gynecologist to rule out any serious causes. The gynecologists at Riverview Health in Noblesville, IN, are here to treat your irregular periods or abnormal vaginal bleeding.

Common Causes of Abnormal Vaginal Bleeding

Some possible causes of abnormal vaginal bleeding can include:

Cervical infections or cancer
Endometrial polyps or cancer
Vaginal cancer
Hyperthyroidism
Hypothyroidism
Polycystic ovarian syndrome
Birth control pills
Ectopic pregnancy
Chlamydia
Gonorrhea
Pelvic inflammatory disease (PID)
Uterine fibroids
Threatened miscarriage
