Meet our Breast Surgeons

We are excited to welcome our newest breast surgeons Monet Bowling, MD and Anne Mattingly, MD. Their expertise and dedication to advancing breast health care will further strengthen our commitment to delivering exceptional, patient-centered surgical services. With their expertise and passion, we are expanding our ability to serve our community and elevate the standard of breast health services at Riverview Health.

Monet Bowling, MD

Dr. Monet Bowling is a board-certified Breast Surgical Oncologist specializing in breast cancer treatment. She earned her medical degree at Indiana University School of Medicine and completed her residency at Indiana University Medical Center. Dr. Bowling further specialized by completing a fellowship at the University of Texas Southwestern Medical School at Dallas. She is certified by the American Board of Surgery.

Dr. Bowling's philosophy of care extends beyond the patient to encompass the entire family, ensuring everyone's needs are addressed. She is dedicated to providing a seamless continuum of care from diagnosis through treatment, working collaboratively with a multidisciplinary team. Her approach is rooted in delivering high-quality, highly personalized care, and she strives to build a personal connection with each patient.

Anne Mattingly, MD

Dr. Anne Mattingly is a board-certified Breast Surgical Oncologist with a passion for providing comprehensive, patient-centered care. She earned her medical degree and completed her residency at Indiana University School of Medicine. Following her residency, she completed a specialized fellowship at H. Lee Moffitt Cancer Center. She is certified by the American Board of Surgery.
