Gift Shop

Whether you're looking for flowers or a gift to cheer up a loved one or you are shopping the latest fashions for yourself, Carolyn's Corner at Riverview Health in Noblesville has what you are looking for. In addition to clothes, jewelry and snacks, we carry personal hygiene items and technology accessories.

For your convenience, we can even deliver items—such as flowers or toothpaste—to patient rooms.

Please note ICU will not accept flowers for patients, you can order flowers for patient rooms.

Where: Located on the second floor near Starbucks Bistro in the atrium of our Noblesville hospital.

Hours: Monday-Friday, 9 a.m. to 4 p.m. Closed on weekends.

Contact Carolyn's Corner

Phone: 317.776.7135

Call ahead to ask about current merchandise, check on flower availability or arrange a delivery to a patient room. The shop team can confirm whether a particular unit is able to receive flowers before you place an order.

Sending a Gift to a Patient

Ordering through Carolyn's Corner is a simple way to brighten a hospital stay when you can't stop by in person. Choose an item, provide the patient's name and room number, and the shop will handle delivery within the Noblesville hospital. Because the ICU does not accept flowers, consider a snack, a small comfort item or a technology accessory for patients on that unit instead.

If you'd also like to send a written note, you can submit a patient message online and a Riverview Health volunteer will hand-deliver it to the room.

Finding the Shop During Your Visit

Carolyn's Corner sits on the second floor of the Noblesville hospital atrium, near the Starbucks Bistro and close to the outpatient lab and imaging at the atrium's west end — Entrance 1 is the closest way in. Since the shop closes at 4 p.m. on weekdays and is closed on weekends, visitors who need snacks, beverages or personal hygiene items outside those hours can use The Market, the hospital's 24/7 self-service store described on our food and drink page. For entrance hours and visiting guidelines, see visitor information.
