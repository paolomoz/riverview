As a county-owned hospital, Riverview Health serves the needs of all individuals in its community.

Riverview Health continually identifies health priorities for those in Hamilton County and works to provide solutions aimed at keeping the community healthy. Each year, Riverview Health provides millions of dollars in uncompensated care.

Riverview Health offers a number of free educational classes and seminars, providing valuable health information to people in the community. We also partner with other healthcare institutions to provide easy access to healthcare resources. View our Classes & Events.

As a part of Employer Wellness Services at Riverview Health, we provide wellness and community programs that assist individuals in leading healthier, more productive lives. Riverview Health is committed to helping individuals, families, businesses and organizations maximize health and wellness at home and at work, creating a healthier community.

As one of the largest employers in Hamilton County, we’re passionate about our community and aim to improve the well-being of those we serve. Each year, we provide solutions designed to keep our community healthy.

Programs and services include:

  • Community festivals and events
  • Diabetes self-management classes
  • Fitness classes
  • Health and wellness educational seminars
  • Support groups
