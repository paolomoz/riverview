Hi, I’m Jason Hemmerling, and this is my recovery story from colon cancer. It’s not an easy topic to talk about, but after everything I have been through, I feel it is important to share my journey. My experience with colon cancer, the care I received, and my path to recovery have truly changed my life.

The care I received from Riverview Health was incredible. From the moment I was diagnosed, the team guided me through every step. I never felt alone. The doctors, nurses, and support staff were compassionate and provided me with the strength I needed to fight this disease.

Thanks to early detection and the treatment I received, I can now go back to my normal life. My recovery was a journey, but it was worth every step. If there’s one thing I’ve learned, it’s the importance of having a care team you can trust. It truly can save your life. Our family is forever grateful to Dr. Samer Kawak and the entire team at Riverview Health for giving me a new life.

Additional Information

Colon cancer is one of the most common cancers in the world, but it is also one of the most preventable types of cancer. How? Colonoscopy—a routine screening test—is key in detecting early signs of cancer and other serious conditions in the colon before they become life-threatening. If you’re over 45 or 50, or have risk factors like a family history of colorectal cancer, colonoscopy should be part of your health routine.

A colonoscopy isn't just another test—it's a life-saving procedure. It can detect colon cancer at its earliest and most treatable stages, often before symptoms even appear. Regular screenings also help prevent cancer by identifying and removing precancerous polyps before they develop into advanced cancer. Early detection and intervention can make all the difference.
