Parking at Riverview Health Noblesville Hospital

Riverview Health Noblesville Hospital is located at 395 Westfield Rd., Noblesville, IN 46060. Parking lots are arranged around the numbered hospital entrances, so the most convenient place to park depends on where you are headed and what time you arrive. The parking lot map above shows each lot and its nearest entrance.

Patients and visitors may use the following entrances:

  • Entrance 1 (ER): Open 24/7. Park here for the emergency room, and note that the outpatient lab, imaging and Carolyn's Corner gift shop at the west end of the atrium are also closest to this entrance.
  • Entrance 2 (Visitors): Open 8 a.m. to 8 p.m. daily. This is the main visitor entrance and the closest one to the hospital cafeteria at the east end of the atrium.
  • Entrance 3 (Women's Pavilion): Open 7 a.m. to 8 p.m., Monday-Friday.
  • Entrance 5 (Outpatient Pharmacy): Park here if you are picking up a prescription from the Riverview Health Outpatient Pharmacy.

Because visiting hours run from 8 a.m. to 8 p.m. every day, most visitors will find Entrance 2 the simplest choice. If you arrive outside those hours for emergency care, Entrance 1 is always open.

Parking at Riverview Health Westfield Hospital

Riverview Health Westfield Hospital is located at 17600 Shamrock Blvd., Westfield, IN 46074. Visitors should park near and enter through the main entrance. For turn-by-turn directions to either campus, see the details on each hospital's location page: Riverview Health Noblesville Hospital and Riverview Health Westfield Hospital.
