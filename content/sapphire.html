Riverview Health and SIHO Insurance Services have partnered to offer a valuable health insurance solution for Central Indiana.

Our vision is to provide local and affordable health care benefits by bringing together a high-quality and coordinated health system and an experienced health insurance carrier committed to controlling cost and providing an excellent customer experience to members.

Sapphire by Riverview Health At Work is a comprehensive health plan, designed by a dedicated team of healthcare providers for Central Indiana.

Healthcare is complex and challenging. Some of the challenges can be removed by simply having less people involved. Sapphire allows businesses to partner directly with the source, streamlining the process along the way. Riverview also guaranteed competitive pricing in designing this plan which offers better discounts for services to members and employers.

It’s even better. Riverview Health has been serving Hamilton County since 1909 and has a long history of providing high quality care for local employers. For services we don’t currently offer, we have a careful complementary selection process for organizations we choose to partner with to ensure you have a comprehensive plan that covers all your bases.

The plan is administered by SIHO. SIHO is headquartered in Columbus, Indiana with an office on the north side of Indianapolis. They have been serving central Indiana since 1987. Like Riverview Health, SIHO believes in doing the little things exceptionally well, while removing barriers to provide high quality care.

Healthcare is complex enough. The last thing you need while navigating your benefit plan is to be bounced around global call centers and endless phone trees. When you call a representative of Sapphire, you’ll be greeted by a person who’s local.Riverview Health and SIHO partner together to answer plan questions, provide support and work with employers to maximize the value of their plan benefit offering. We have a team of local professionals dedicated to providing exceptional service every step of the way.

Our plan is available to all size employers, with flexibility and options available that work best for you

In addition to our comprehensive health plan, we can provide add-on services such as fitness center memberships at our Noblesville and Carmel Rehabilitation & Fitness Centers or participation in our Top Talent program for companies looking to bolster their total rewards package.
