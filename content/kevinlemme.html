About Kevin Lemme, MD

Kevin Lemme, MD, FAAOS, is a board-certified orthopedic surgeon who completed his training at Indiana University School of Medicine. With 18 years of experience, he specializes in total joint replacement, hip and knee arthritis treatment, and general orthopedics, with a focus on relieving pain and restoring mobility through both conservative and surgical care.

Dr. Lemme is dedicated to treating each patient as a whole person, helping them return to the activities they love and maintain a healthy, active lifestyle. He also has a strong interest in fracture care and is actively involved in his local community, where he lives with his four children.
