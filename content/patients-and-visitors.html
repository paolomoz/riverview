Preventative Screenings

  • Computed Tomography (CT) Heart Scan Get a heart scan done at $49 today.
  • Colonoscopy Colorectal cancer screening is recommended for adults aged 45 to 75, regardless of gender. Schedule Online.
  • Vascular Screening Package Schedule vascular screenings online by answering our questionnaire.
  • Mammograms Mammograms are recommended annually for women starting at age 40. Answer our questionnaire and schedule online.
  • Direct Access Lab Testing We offer direct access lab testing. No referral required.
  • Lung Cancer Screening with Low-dose CT This screening uses a CT scan to identify early signs of lung cancer. Healthcare Provider's Order Required.
