Bray Society

Throughout their more than 40 years of support to Riverview Health, the Brays served as volunteers, members of the Foundation board and strong philanthropists who cared about education and our community. Dr. Bray was a scientist at Eli Lilly and Co. and a member of the Foundation Board of Directors for 18 years. Mrs. Bray volunteered at Riverview Health for more than 40 years and her great-grandfather was instrumental in relocating Riverview Health to its location today.

The Bray Society is a tribute to Dr. Malcolm and Mary Bray, who have a long heritage of service and giving to Riverview Health. Bray Society members have made contributions ranging from $10,000 to $249,999 to Riverview Health Foundation. Bray Society members promote health, life and hope at Riverview Health.

Bray Society members are invited to a special recognition event and receive a naming opportunity as well as a feature story in Riverviews. The Bray Society members’ names are also displayed on the recognition wall in the Riverview Health Atrium.

To join our list of members, contact Riverview Health Foundation at 317.776.7317 or mwiles@riverview.org.
