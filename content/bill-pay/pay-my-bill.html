Pay My Bill

Paying your Riverview Health bill is easy with our convenient online payment form. We're here to answer any questions you may have about your bill or financial assistance options. We never want you to have any billing surprises at Riverview Health. That's why we offer an online cost estimator request tool. Simply enter the required information into the form, and one of our billing representatives will reach out to you with a personalized cost estimate.

Are you uninsured or underinsured? Riverview Health partners with The Well Fund to help connect those in need with coverage and assistance. Visit www.riverview.org/mymcc.io where you can easily and quickly screen and enroll in health coverage for you and your entire household.

In addition, patients may make payment arrangements prior to dismissal by contacting one of our Financial Representatives at 317.286.6575 or 800.523.6001.

The following forms and documents are additional resources for our Financial Assistance Program:

Our staff can help you finalize your financial arrangements or explain the billing procedures. If you need help with payment of your bill or have questions concerning your bill, please contact one of our financial representatives at 317.286.6575 or 800.523.6001. Also, check out the Billing & Insurance Frequently Asked Questions.

For help with your Riverview Health Physicians account, please contact Riverview Health Physicians Patient Accounts at 317.770.6900.

You can also learn more in our glossary of billing terms.

A daily room charge includes fees for the room, 24-hour nursing care, meals, housekeeping, maintenance and laundry services—all of which are provided for your comfort and to guard you against infection and injury. Special services ordered by your physician, such as an operating room, pharmacy, X-rays, laboratory tests, anesthesia, physical therapy, respiratory therapy and other diagnostic treatment procedures, will appear as extra charges on your bill.

Often, you will get more than one bill because separate bills are issued by Riverview Health and by individual physicians involved with your care, such as your surgeon and radiologist. Your hospital bill doesn’t include the fees of your attending physician, surgeon, anesthesiologist, pathologist, radiologist or consulting physician. You will be billed directly by their offices. We will bill your insurance company if you provide us with the necessary information.

For more information about the cost of care, please visit mycareINsight, a website was created by the Indiana Hospital Association to inform health care consumers about various aspects of pricing as well as quality information.
