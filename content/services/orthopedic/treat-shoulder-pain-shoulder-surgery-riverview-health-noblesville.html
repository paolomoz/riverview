Shoulder Repair with the Orthopedic Specialists at Riverview Health

If you suffer from a painful shoulder problem, talk to one of our doctors about the types of shoulder surgeries available, including joint replacement, rotator cuff repair and arthroscopy. Surgery could be the best option if more conservative treatments, such as physical therapy and medications, haven’t been effective.

Shoulder surgery can be as simple as a minimally invasive arthroscopic procedure or a mini-open procedure, or as extensive as an open surgery. Mini-open and arthroscopic procedures use smaller incisions, often resulting in less pain and a shorter recovery time.
