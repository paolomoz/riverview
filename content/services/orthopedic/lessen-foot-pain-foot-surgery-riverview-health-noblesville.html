What to Expect from Foot Surgery at Riverview Health

General anesthesia is applied before a foot treatment, so you won’t feel anything during the procedure. Most foot surgeries are outpatient procedures, and you can expect to go home the same day.

The podiatry specialists at Riverview Health may suggest the following during your foot surgery recovery:

  • Compression
  • Elevation
  • Ice
  • Physical therapy
  • Rehabilitation
  • Rest
  • Stretches and exercises to do at home
  • Wearing a cast or boot for stability

Your doctor will give you individualized instructions on how to help your foot heal properly and return to normal activities.
