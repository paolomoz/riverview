Lessen Foot Pain with Foot Surgery at Riverview Health in Noblesville, IN

Schedule an Appointment

Podiatrists and foot surgeons recommend surgery for people who have foot problems that can treated effectively with surgical methods. If you have a foot condition that hasn’t improved with conservative treatments, talk to the specialists at Riverview Health about foot surgery.

The podiatry experts at Riverview Health provide several types of foot surgeries to treat a variety of foot conditions:

  • Bunion surgery realigns the big toe joint with the rest of the foot
  • Foot fusion surgery relieves arthritis pain by permanently joining bones together
  • Hammertoe surgery straightens an unaligned toe
  • Metatarsal surgery can fix dislocated or deformed toes
  • Neuroma surgery stops pain from thickened tissue around the nerves between the bases of toes
  • Plantar fascia release is a type of heel surgery that relieves heel pain caused by plantar fasciitis
  • Tendon surgery reroutes or repairs a foot tendon to improve movement

What to Expect from Foot Surgery at Riverview Health

General anesthesia is applied before a foot treatment, so you won’t feel anything during the procedure. Most foot surgeries are outpatient procedures, and you can expect to go home the same day.

The podiatry specialists at Riverview Health may suggest the following during your foot surgery recovery:

  • Compression
  • Elevation
  • Ice
  • Physical therapy
  • Rehabilitation
  • Rest
  • Stretches and exercises to do at home
  • Wearing a cast or boot for stability

Your doctor will give you individualized instructions on how to help your foot heal properly and return to normal activities.

If you’d like to see if foot surgery is right for you, reach out to Riverview Health by calling 317.565.0505 or by completing an appointment request form.
