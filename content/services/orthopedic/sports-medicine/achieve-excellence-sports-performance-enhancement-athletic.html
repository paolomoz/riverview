Athletic Training with Riverview Health

Certified athletic trainers at Riverview Health provide healthy training for all types of athletes, including middle and high school athletes and weekend warriors. Our team of experts can teach you the best ways to care for your body while playing sports, such as instructions for stretches, post-workout care, injury prevention, emergency treatment and more.

If you experience a sports-related injury, Riverview Health offers a variety of treatments for pain, including physical therapy, rehabilitation and surgery.

Complete a Sports Enhancement Program

Our Sports Enhancement program helps identify an athlete’s health and physical needs in order to maximize his or her performance. Riverview Health offers a number of tests for athletes, including:

Anemia (iron panel and HGB)
Complete blood count
Diabetic A1c
Glucose
Heart health (liipids, hs-CRP and homocysteine)
Hemoglobin (HGB)
High-sensitivity CRP
Homocysteine
Kidney function (BMP)
Liver function
Prostate (PSA)
Thyroid (TSH and free T4)
Vitamin B12/folate
