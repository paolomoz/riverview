Herniated Disc Treatment &amp; Recovery

Disc removal can be a minimally invasive procedure with a small surgical incision. The length of your hospital stay depends on the type of surgery, but typically is performed outpatient. Your condition should improve over the first few weeks, and a full recovery may take up to six weeks to several months.

After your disc removal surgery, you’ll have to take precautions to prevent future back or neck problems. Maintaining a healthy weight can relieve pressure on your spine, and you should avoid activities that stress your back and neck, such as gardening. Also, you should use correct body mechanics when lifting heavy items.
