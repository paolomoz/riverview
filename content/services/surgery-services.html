Surgery Services

Schedule an Appointment

Whether your surgical needs are minor or complex, the surgery team at Riverview Health has the expertise and technology to provide advanced care in a convenient, comfortable and efficient setting.

Here, you have access to a comprehensive range of surgery services, including a full-scale inpatient and outpatient laboratory and pharmacy, advanced radiology and cardiology departments, and other specialists should an emergency arise.

Our board-certified surgeons combine their training in the latest techniques with today’s newest technologies to perform even the most complex procedures, including minimally invasive surgery.

Our staff of highly skilled doctors at Hamilton Surgical Associates commonly performs procedures such as breast surgery, abdominal hernia repair, gallbladder removal, surgery of the colon, placement of vascular access devices, vasectomies and surgery of the chest.

With colon and rectal specialists on our team, we are proud to offer:

  • Conservative and surgical treatment of hemorrhoids, fissures, anorectal pain and bleeding
  • Medical treatment of constipation, diarrhea and irritable bowel syndrome
  • Colonoscopies for screening, diagnosis, or surveillance
  • Anorectal disease care
  • Colorectal cancer care
  • Pelvic floor dysfunction issues
  • Robotic Surgery
