Treatment for Pelvic Pain in Noblesville, IN

Pelvic pain occurs when dull or sharp pain is felt in the lower abdomen or pelvis. Depending on the person, pelvic pain can present as constant or come in waves during certain activities. Occasionally, pelvic pain can be felt in the lower back or thighs.Rely on the expertise at Riverview Health in Noblesville, IN, to diagnose and find the right treatment for your pelvic pain. Our gynecologists are highly trained in diagnosing and treating this condition—and we’re committed to helping you return to a pain-free life.

What Causes Pelvic Pain?

Endometriosis

Menstrual cramps

Ectopic pregnancy

Ovarian cysts

Ovarian cancer

Pelvic inflammatory disease (PID)

Uterine fibroids

Appendicitis

Crohn’s disease/ulcerative colitis

Adhesions from previous abdominal or pelvic surgery

Kidney stones

Urinary tract infection (UTI)
