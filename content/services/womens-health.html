At Riverview Health, we’re passionate about personalizing healthcare and meeting the unique needs of women. We combine a team of experienced physicians, specialists and staff with the latest technologies to offer comprehensive women’s health services close to home. Helping you understand your healthcare options and providing sound advice so you can make decisions that are right for you is a top priority.

From adolescence through mid-life and beyond, board certified Riverview Health OB/GYN physicians provide unmatched gynecological care. Service offerings range from routine exams to the diagnosis and treatment of more pressing women’s health issues.

From family planning and birth control to mammography and menopause, we're here to help you navigate your health. For women who are expecting a baby, Riverview Health Maternity provides a full spectrum of services and amenities including spa-like labor, delivery and recovery suites, on-site surgical rooms, and breastfeeding support provided by certified lactation consultants.

If you already have children, we understand the health and well-being of your little ones are as important as your own. The experienced pediatric hospitalists and the staff of the pediatric unit at Riverview Health are dedicated exclusively to meeting the unique medical needs of children—giving you a peace of mind.

In addition to routine and preventive care, Riverview Health provides a wide array of specialty care delivered by our highly trained experts. Whatever need may arise, we’ve got you covered. Women have access to a full spectrum of heart & vascular, orthopedic, breast care, diabetes & endocrinology, gastroenterology and urology services all in one convenient location.

Teamed up with our primary care physicians and other specialists, the experts at Riverview Health can diagnose and treat other common conditions women face such as:

Gallstones
Heartburn/GERD
Kidney stones
Osteoporosis
Over-active bladder
Peptic ulcers
Urinary tract infections
Vaginitis

We also offer help achieving and maintaining a healthy body through our Body Knowledge program. Body Knowledge is a body composition and weight management program that takes advantage of the accurate body composition measurements of a BOD POD.

Ariane Spidel’s Story: Routine checkup matters!

For Ariane Spidel, a routine check-up became a pivotal moment that led to the early detection of breast cancer and a journey of strength, resilience, and expert care. Diagnosed with ductal carcinoma in situ, Arane underwent a lumpectomy, radiation therapy, and continues with medical oncology, supported every step of the way by the compassionate and coordinated team at Riverview Health.
