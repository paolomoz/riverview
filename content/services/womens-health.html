Ariane Spidel’s Story: Routine checkup matters!

Diagnosed with ductal carcinoma in situ, Arane underwent a lumpectomy, radiation therapy, and continues with medical oncology, supported every step of the way by the compassionate and coordinated team at Riverview Health.
