Riverview Health has two convenient outpatient pharmacies—one in Noblesville located inside Riverview Health Surgery Pavilion and one in Westfield on the first floor of Riverview Health Westfield Hospital.

Prescription fulfillment is available to Riverview Health employees and their dependents and patients of Riverview Health who are being treated by Riverview Health for the condition related to their prescription, including patients who have been discharged from Riverview Health or have had a surgical or other outpatient procedure at Riverview Health. Specific eligibility for prescriptions will be determined by Riverview Health outpatient pharmacies.

Online Pharmacy Patient Portal

With our online pharmacy patient portal, managing and refilling your prescriptions is easy. We’ll even send you notifications via text message, phone or email so you’ll never miss a refill.

Filling and Transferring Prescriptions

To fill a new prescription, ask your Riverview Health provider to send it electronically to the Noblesville or Westfield pharmacy, then pick it up during the pharmacy hours listed above. If you would like to move an existing prescription from another pharmacy, bring your current prescription bottle or label and our pharmacy team will handle the transfer request for you. Having your insurance information ready when you arrive helps the process go smoothly.

Questions About Your Medications

Our pharmacists do more than dispense. They can review the medications you take—including over-the-counter products and supplements—screen for drug interactions, and offer counseling on how and when to take each one. If a medication's cost is a concern, they will work with your healthcare provider to identify cost-effective alternatives that still meet your needs. For a scheduled, in-depth review of everything you take, see our medication therapy management services, where you can also request an appointment.
