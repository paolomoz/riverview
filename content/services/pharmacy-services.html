Riverview Health has two convenient outpatient pharmacies—one in Noblesville located inside Riverview Health Surgery Pavilion and one in Westfield on the first floor of Riverview Health Westfield Hospital.

Prescription fulfillment is available to Riverview Health employees and their dependents and patients of Riverview Health who are being treated by Riverview Health for the condition related to their prescription, including patients who have been discharged from Riverview Health or have had a surgical or other outpatient procedure at Riverview Health. Specific eligibility for prescriptions will be determined by Riverview Health outpatient pharmacies.

Online Pharmacy Patient Portal

With our online pharmacy patient portal, managing and refilling your prescriptions is easy. We’ll even send you notifications via text message, phone or email so you’ll never miss a refill.
