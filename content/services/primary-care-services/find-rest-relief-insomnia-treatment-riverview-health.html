What Causes Insomnia?

Insomnia can be caused by a variety of factors. It’s often a symptom of another problem, such as certain medical conditions, medication side effects or other sleep disorders.

Causes of insomnia can include:

  • Behavior factors, including changing sleep schedules, too much caffeine or lack of exercise
  • Circadian rhythm disorder
  • Sleep settings like an uncomfortable mattress, noise or hot or cold temperatures
  • Stress, such as problems at work, money worries or family events
