We also offer specialized ENT services—including ear tubes—for more complex otolaryngology conditions, infections, allergies, cancer and hearing and speech problems. Find an otolaryngologist near you to address your ear, nose and throat problems.

ENT Treatments Available at Riverview Health in Noblesville, IN

Ear problems: Ear infections, hearing loss, ringing in the ears (tinnitus), balance disorders, cholesteatoma (abnormal skin growth in the ear), noncancerous growths and congenital disorders, and outer and inner ear deformities

Head and neck problems: Salivary gland problems, sleep apnea and facial irregularities or deformities

Nose problems: Sinusitis (sinus infection), deviated septum, nasal polyps, loss of smell and nosebleeds

Throat problems: GERD (gastroesophageal reflux disease), tonsil and adenoid infections, laryngitis, voice and swallowing disorders, and strep throat
