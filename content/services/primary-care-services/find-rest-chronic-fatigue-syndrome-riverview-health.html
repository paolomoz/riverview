Find Rest from Chronic Fatigue Syndrome at Riverview Health

Home Services

Chronic fatigue syndrome is a disorder that’s characterized by constant, extreme fatigue. While it isn’t explained by a particular medical condition, it can be caused by a variety of underlying factors. Chronic fatigue syndrome can be managed to help you live a productive life.

Your Riverview Health primary care physician will evaluate you to determine if your fatigue is caused by a treatable medical condition, such as anemia, thyroid problems, or cardiovascular disease.

Why am I Always Tired?

Chronic fatigue syndrome symptoms often include:

  • Anxiety
  • Fatigue
  • Forgetfulness
  • Lack of concentration
  • Pain that moves from joint to joint without swelling or redness
  • Restless sleep
  • Sore throat
  • Unexplained muscle pain

Management & Treatment for Chronic Fatigue Syndrome in Noblesville, IN & Surrounding Areas

At Riverview Health, our physicians can help you find ways to manage chronic fatigue syndrome. To feel more energized, we recommend:

  • Avoiding caffeine and alcohol
  • Eating healthy foods
  • Exercising
  • Drinking plenty of water
  • Forming a consistent sleep pattern by going to bed and waking up at the same time every day
  • Relaxing before you try to sleep by taking a bath or reading

If you’ve had constant fatigue for six months or more, find a primary care physician at Riverview Health to help find a solution for you. Treatments can include medication, stress management, relaxation techniques and therapy. Our primary care physicians can recommend the best treatment for you or refer to you a specialist if necessary.
