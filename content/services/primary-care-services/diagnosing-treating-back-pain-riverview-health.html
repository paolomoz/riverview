Causes of Back Pain

Back pain can be caused by a number of conditions that vary in severity. Together, our primary care and orthopedic doctors take steps to diagnose your back pain by evaluating your symptoms, reviewing your medical history and providing a physical exam. We may also suggest imaging tests like X-rays.

Causes of back pain can include:

  • Arthritis
  • Disc problems
  • Muscle strain or spasms caused by overuse or injury
  • Osteoporosis
  • Sciatic nerve pain
  • Scoliosis
