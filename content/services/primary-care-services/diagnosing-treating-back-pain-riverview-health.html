Diagnosing & Treating Back Pain at Riverview Health

Home Services

Whether your back pain is acute or chronic, or located in your upper or lower back, it can be frustrating and affect your daily life. At Riverview Health in Noblesville, IN, our primary care physicians work together with our orthopedic specialists to accurately diagnose the cause of your back pain and provide expert treatment options that meet your needs with precision and care.

Causes of Back Pain

Back pain can be caused by a number of conditions that vary in severity. Together, our primary care and orthopedic doctors take steps to diagnose your back pain by evaluating your symptoms, reviewing your medical history and providing a physical exam. We may also suggest imaging tests like X-rays.

Causes of back pain can include:

  • Arthritis
  • Disc problems
  • Muscle strain or spasms caused by overuse or injury
  • Osteoporosis
  • Sciatic nerve pain
  • Scoliosis

Exceptional Back Pain Treatment in Noblesville, IN & Surrounding Areas

After we diagnose the cause of your back pain, we work together to provide a personalized care plan. Our primary care providers can prescribe pain medication, as well as recommend a physical therapist if needed. We may also suggest diagnostic and therapeutic injections from our professional pain management specialists.

If your back problem is more severe, Riverview Health provides innovative back surgeries to help you lead a pain-free life, such as:

Cervical and lumbar decompression
Disc replacement
Herniated disc surgery
Kyphoplasty
Minimally invasive surgery
Spinal cord stimulation
Spinal fusion
