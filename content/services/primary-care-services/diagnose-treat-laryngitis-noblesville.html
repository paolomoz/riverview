Diagnose & Treat Laryngitis in Noblesville, IN

Home Services

Laryngitis is the inflammation of your larynx, or voice box. This throat condition can also be called laryngeal inflammation. It’s only contagious if caused by an infection.

Laryngitis symptoms typically involve:

A hoarse, raspy or deep voice
Coughing, especially a dry cough
Dry or sore throat
Loss of voice
Trouble swallowing

At Riverview Health, our primary care providers and otolaryngologists provide expertise in laryngitis treatment in Noblesville, IN and surrounding areas.

What Causes Laryngitis?

Causes of laryngitis can include:

Acid reflux
Colds or the flu
Irritation from allergies or smoking
Laryngeal cancer
Speaking or singing incorrectly
Voice overuse

Treatment for Laryngitis at Riverview Health

When you come to a Riverview Health primary care location, your physician will perform a physical exam to help determine the cause of the laryngitis. If needed, he or she will refer you to an ENT specialist.

Laryngitis can usually be treated at home by resting your voice or speaking softly if needed. Drink plenty of fluids, and avoid smoking and secondhand smoke. If your symptoms haven’t improved in a few weeks, contact your primary care physician for further evaluation.

If your laryngitis is caused by acid reflux or a problem with the way you talk or sing, taking steps to reduce your reflux or change your speaking or singing technique may help.
