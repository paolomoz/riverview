Asthma causes swelling and narrowing in your lung airways. While no one knows the exact causes of asthma, it’s believed to be a combination of genetic and environmental factors. When you have asthma, you have tightened airway muscles, swollen airways linings and increased mucus in your lungs.

Through our primary care services, Riverview Health offers treatment for asthma in Noblesville, IN and surrounding areas. Reach out to a doctor near you to help manage your asthma and reduce its effects on your daily life.

Asthma Triggers & Symptoms

Asthma can be triggered by a number of items or situations, including:

  • Allergens like animals, dust, pollen and mold
  • Cold-related illnesses
  • Emotions, such as laughing, stress or crying
  • Exercising
  • Irritants, including smoke, strong odors, household cleaners and air pollution
  • Medicines
  • Weather changes

Triggers cause your airways to flare up, and these asthma attacks can be chronic or range from moderate to severe.

Chronic or moderate symptoms of asthma can involve:

  • Chest tightness
  • Coughing
  • Low energy
  • Shortness of breath
  • Wheezing

A severe flare-up can be life threatening, as it can be difficult to breathe.

Find Effective Asthma Treatment in Noblesville, IN

Asthma is a long-term condition, and it’s important to partner with your healthcare provider to manage your symptoms, reduce attacks and avoid emergency situations. We recommend avoiding your asthma triggers and taking your medication as instructed, even if your asthma isn’t acting up.

We offer a variety of treatments for asthma, such as:

  • Bronchodilators or airway openers, which relax the muscles around airways
  • Inhalers
  • Nebulizers
  • Steroids or anti-inflammatory drugs
