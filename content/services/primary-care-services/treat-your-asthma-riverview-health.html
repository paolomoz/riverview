Treat Your Asthma at Riverview Health

Asthma causes swelling and narrowing in your lung airways. While no one knows the exact causes of asthma, it’s believed to be a combination of genetic and environmental factors. When you have asthma, you have tightened airway muscles, swollen airways linings and increased mucus in your lungs.
