Signs & Symptoms of Diabetes

While the cause of Type 1 diabetes is unknown, we know that Type 2 diabetes occurs when your body resists the action of insulin, which leads to high blood sugar.

Depending on your condition, symptoms of diabetes may include:

  • Blurry vision
  • Feeling fatigued
  • Feeling increasingly thirsty
  • Increased appetite
  • Slowly healing sores
  • Urinating frequently
  • Weight loss

Diabetes Treatments at Riverview Health

  • Lifestyle counseling

Diabetes Education & Support Groups

Getting an initial diagnosis of diabetes can be scary and confusing. Your primary care doctor may refer you to the Diabetes Education Center at Riverview Health. When you visit the Diabetes Education Center, our certified diabetes educators can provide treatment information and prevention techniques to help you achieve better health. You can also count on the support of a community with our diabetes support group. Here, you’ll find opportunities to share and connect with others who are living with diabetes. Our monthly support group will help you find the best care and treatments for your individual needs.
