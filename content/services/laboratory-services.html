Laboratory Services

Schedule an Appointment

Upcoming medical procedures or physicals often require blood testing done by an accredited laboratory. At Riverview Health, our certified phlebotomists and technologists provide a full range of laboratory services, paired with technical excellence and personal care.

Riverview Health is licensed by the State of Indiana, and is certified by the College of American Pathologists (CAP) and the American Association of Blood Banks (AABB).

Riverview Health Noblesville Hospital accreditation certificates:

  • CLIA Licensure
  • CAP Certificate
  • AABB Certificate

Riverview Health Westfield Hospital accreditation certificates:

  • CLIA Licensure
  • CAP Certificate

Our full-service laboratory provides clinical testing services for, 24 hours a day, seven days a week. And we serve as a reference laboratory for doctors’ offices and extended care facilities throughout central Indiana, where specially trained couriers pick up samples daily and deliver them to our laboratory for testing. If you’re a Riverview Health Physicians patient, your results are sent directly to your medical record for quick access by your doctor.

Riverview Health offers a selection of direct-to-consumer laboratory health profiles through its Direct Access Laboratory Testing.

Direct Access Laboratory Testing

With our Direct Access Laboratory, you can order and pay for lab tests online. These test profiles are designed to evaluate your current health status via a simple blood draw done at your convenience at either our Noblesville or Westfield hospital locations. The profiles are available at a discounted price and a physician’s order is not required.

Tests We Provide

At Riverview Health, we offer over 400 laboratory tests performed on-site at our Noblesville and Westfield locations. Because most tests are performed right here in Hamilton County, you receive results faster—without the delays that come from sending specimens to out-of-state labs.
