Coordinated Care From Referral to Follow-Up

Specialty care at Riverview Health usually begins with a conversation. In many cases, your primary care provider identifies a concern and refers you to the appropriate specialist; for many of the specialties listed above, you can also request an appointment directly. Because our specialists practice within one health system, visit notes, test results and treatment plans are shared with the rest of your care team instead of being scattered across unrelated offices.

Finding the Right Specialist

Browse our provider directory to review the physicians and advanced practice providers behind each specialty, or explore our locations in Noblesville, Westfield and across Hamilton County to find an office close to home. If you are not sure which specialty fits your symptoms, start with your primary care office—they can point you to the right department and help arrange any lab work or imaging needed before your first specialty visit.

After your appointment, you can review results, message your care team and manage follow-up visits through MyChart.
