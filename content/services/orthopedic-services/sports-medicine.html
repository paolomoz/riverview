Sports Medicine

The setback of a sports injury can be devastating. At Riverview Health Physicians Orthopedics & Sports Medicine, our goal is to get you back to your pre-injury condition as quickly and safely as possible.

Schedule an Appointment

The sports medicine physicians at Riverview Health are board-certified and specialize in the diagnosis and treatment of problems associated with bones, joints, muscles and nerves. Using a comprehensive team approach, our experts treat and assist with the rehabilitation of sports and exercise-related issues, and work with patients to prevent further injuries.

Rehabilitation Care

If your sports medicine physician refers you to one of our licensed physical therapists, you’ll receive excellent care from an experienced therapist who’s specifically trained in using evidence-based rehabilitation techniques to quickly restore your motion, strength and overall function. The physical therapist and sports medicine physician will collaborate throughout your recovery process to ensure the best possible outcome.
