Riverview Health is committed to providing high-quality corporate wellness services with a strong emphasis on health education and long-term behavior change using best practices, guidelines and research. We value our clients and participants in everything we do. This philosophy means we strive to understand the health needs of each client’s population, and we design our services to meet your needs. We take pride in delivering our services in a caring, efficient manner.

Services We Provide

  • Biometric screenings and "Know Your Numbers" events
  • Corporate wellness challenges
  • Ergonomic and safety assessments
  • Fitness programs
  • Flu shot clinics
  • Incentive program maintenance and support
  • Mammography programs
  • Nutritional counseling by registered dietitians
  • On-site lunch-and-learn presentations
  • On-site one-on-one employee wellness coaching
  • Weight management programming and body composition tracking
  • Wellness program design and consulting

Building a Program Around Your Workforce

No two employee populations look alike, which is why our wellness program design and consulting work starts with your organization rather than a one-size-fits-all package. Biometric screenings and "Know Your Numbers" events establish a picture of your employees' health; from there, elements such as one-on-one wellness coaching, weight management programming with body composition tracking, nutritional counseling from registered dietitians, and corporate wellness challenges can be combined to match the needs those screenings reveal. Lunch-and-learn presentations, flu shot clinics and mammography programs bring services directly to your workplace, while incentive program maintenance and support helps keep participation strong over time.

Part of Riverview At Work

Corporate wellness is one piece of a broader set of employer services from Riverview Health. Depending on your organization's goals, wellness programming can complement occupational health services such as ergonomic and safety assessments, on-site care through the Riverview Health Employer Health Center, and direct primary care arrangements. Employees looking to stay active can also take advantage of fitness and wellness offerings at Riverview Health.
