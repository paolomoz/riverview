Overview

Whether you have a fracture or a more complex health concern, a precise diagnostic image is one of the most critical steps in receiving an accurate diagnosis. Riverview Health Radiology & Imaging offers the latest radiology and imaging technology, as well as the skilled professionals it takes to properly interpret results.

At Riverview Health, we’ve made access to radiology and imaging services convenient for you by providing a full range of services within one central location. All of your imaging needs can be taken care of in one place, with the skill and care of one team of professionals looking out for your health.

Our radiology and imaging team includes radiologists, imaging technologists and a radiology nurse. We perform more than 55,000 imaging services a year and provide services 24 hours a day, seven days a week—so we’re always here when you need us.

For non-emergency imaging services, you can schedule your exam with an order from your physician. For an unplanned or emergency need, our goal is to provide prompt services within minutes of your registration.

Riverview Health Radiology & Imaging has achieved accreditation from the American College of Radiology (ACR) in Computed Tomography, Magnetic Resonance, Ultrasound and Nuclear Medicine—an achievement that demonstrates our continued commitment to quality care and patient safety.
