Overview

When faced with a cancer diagnosis, having an expert team on your side can make all the difference in the world. Our skilled physicians and other dedicated team members at Riverview Health Cancer Center are here to fight for you every step of the way. We do more than just treat your cancer: we guide, educate and support you through your treatment and beyond.

Getting an accurate diagnosis is the first step in your journey to overcome cancer. Our diagnostic services use advanced technologies such as PET/CT scans to determine the most appropriate treatment plan for your specific needs and cancer type. We work with you to select the best course of action, taking into account your physical health and concerns for recovery.

Riverview Health Cancer Center brings together oncologists, surgeons, radiologists and pathologists in one convenient location to ensure you receive the best care possible. Our center provides hematology and oncology services in partnership with Hematology Oncology of Indiana (HOI) and we’re conveniently located across the hall from the radiology department, giving you greater accessibility to treatment. We also offer some of the most advanced treatment technologies in the world, including intensity-modulated radiation therapy (IMRT) for targeted radiation that spares healthy tissue.

When you choose Riverview Health Cancer Center, you’re choosing a team of cancer experts who will support you with expert, compassionate care and a dedication that’s second-to-none.

We’re also proud to partner with Suburban Home Health to offer in-home skilled nursing, home health aides and rehabilitation services to our patients.
