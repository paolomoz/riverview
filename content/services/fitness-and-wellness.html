Fitness Memberships

Riverview Health Rehab & Fitness in Noblesville and Carmel offer monthly fitness memberships and recreational fitness classes for the general public. Our memberships don’t require a long-term contract, and you can try an individual fitness class like aqua aerobics, mat Pilates and boxing fusion before committing to an eight-week session.
