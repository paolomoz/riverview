Rehabilitation Services

Schedule an Appointment

No matter what type of rehabilitation you need, our specialists are here to help you regain your functional independence as quickly and safely as possible, while enhancing your overall quality of life.

We offer advanced physical, occupational and speech therapy, as well as aquatic therapy and concussion management services. In addition, we provide post-therapy and recreational fitness classes to help you stay in shape and improve your overall well-being. We also have an extensive cardiopulmonary rehabilitation program for those recovering from a heart or lung condition.

Our therapy services are provided in a variety of settings, including:

  • RehabCare unit and cardiopulmonary rehabilitation at Riverview Health.
  • Five rehabilitation locations throughout Noblesville and Carmel, including two that provide post-therapy fitness programs.
  • Suburban Home Health, a home health partnership between Riverview Health and Hancock Regional Hospital.
