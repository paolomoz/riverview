Cardiopulmonary Rehab

Schedule an Appointment

We’re pleased to offer an extensive cardiopulmonary rehabilitation program that’s certified by the American Association of Cardiovascular and Pulmonary Rehabilitation. The Cardiopulmonary Rehabilitation program at Riverview Health includes disease management, exercise and education designed to give you the information, tools and confidence you need to live a healthier life.

Led by Dr. Mario Pyles, our cardiac rehabilitation program helps those recovering from a heart attack, heart transplant, open heart surgery, angioplasty or stent placement, as well as those who have coronary artery disease, angina, heart failure or cardiomyopathy. Led by Dr. Ahmad Saltagi, our pulmonary rehabilitation program helps those recovering from COPD, emphysema, chronic bronchitis, asthma, pulmonary fibrosis and bronchiectasis. A doctor referral is required for both programs.
