Find Relief from Morning Sickness at Riverview Health

A common discomfort during pregnancy, morning sickness is characterized by nausea and vomiting. Around 90 percent of women will experience morning sickness at some point while they’re pregnant. This condition is most likely to develop during the first three months of pregnancy (the first trimester).

Morning Sickness Symptoms

The most frequent signs and symptoms of morning sickness are feelings of nausea, fatigue and vomiting.

Trouble keeping liquids down
Periods of fainting or extreme fatigue
Very dark, concentrated urine
Difficulty being able to completely empty your bladder
Nausea that lasts more than a few months

Morning Sickness Causes

Experts are not certain what causes morning sickness, but they do believe it may be related to rising hormone levels during pregnancy.

Prevention & Treatment of Morning Sickness in Noblesville, IN

Having a small amount of food before getting up in the morning may help settle your stomach

Eating smaller meals more frequently

Staying hydrated throughout the day

Getting plenty of fresh air

Eating fruits like lemon or watermelon, as these may help relieve nausea for some women

Avoiding triggers, such as certain foods or smells that cause you to feel nauseous

Including ginger in diet
