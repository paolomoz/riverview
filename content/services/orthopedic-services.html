When an injury or other illness interferes with your ability to move and be active, turn to your local experts in orthopedic care at Riverview Health. Our specialists use advanced surgical and non-surgical techniques, such as cryotherapy, along with a comprehensive range of rehabilitation therapies to diagnose and treat conditions of the bones, joints, muscles and nerves.

Orthopedic problems are often first identified at a visit with your primary care physician. Depending on your doctor’s assessment, you may be referred to one of our orthopedics and sports medicine specialists for a more in-depth evaluation. From arthroscopy to joint replacement, our dedicated team can help identify and implement a treatment plan that best fits your individual needs and mobility goals. In addition to treatment for knees and hips, we also provide surgery to treat carpal tunnel syndrome and problems with your shoulders.

Should you require a total joint replacement, you can trust the orthopedic professionals at Riverview Health to help you get back to doing the things you love most. Our expert team will guide you each step of the way, from your initial consultation to post-surgical therapy and rehabilitation.

Occupational and Physical Therapy

Whether you are post-surgery or suffering from a sports-related injury, occupational and physical therapy can help give you the tools you need for a full recovery. The therapy team at Riverview Health Rehab & Fitness works closely with the orthopedic team to customize care and ensure that you bounce back as quickly and safely as possible.

Sports Medicine for All Ages

We also provide comprehensive care for a wide range of workplace and sports-related injuries and conditions, such as strains, sprains, tears and dislocations. Our surgeons and therapists work together to treat and rehabilitate these injuries—and we even offer restorative services such as aquatic and massage therapy to help set you back in motion.
