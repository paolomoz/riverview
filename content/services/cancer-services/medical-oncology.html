Medical Oncology

Schedule an Appointment

In partnership with Hematology Oncology of Indiana (HOI), Riverview Health has brought hematology and oncology services together in one location—a beautiful new suite within our hospital.

With medical oncology services in one area, our physicians can collaborate more easily to serve people who may be undergoing both types of treatment: disorders of the blood (hematology) and disorders related to tumors (oncology). This allows us to provide you with an even higher level of coordinated care.

While undergoing treatment, you can expect to experience a new level of convenient, compassionate care for all your medical oncology needs in a supportive, comfortable environment. Our suite is uniquely designed with nature elements that promote healing and offers a spacious group setting where people can give and receive support from each other during treatment.

Conditions We Treat

There are hundreds of types of cancer, with some being much more common than others. The following are just some of the types of cancer we can diagnose and treat.

  • Brain tumors
  • Breast cancer
  • Endocrine cancer
  • Gastrointestinal cancer
  • Genitourinary cancer
  • Gynecologic cancer
  • Head and neck cancers
  • Hematologic (blood) cancer
  • Hepato-pancreato-biliary cancer
  • Prostate cancer
  • Sarcoma
  • Skin cancer and melanoma
  • Thoracic (lung) cancer
  • Thymoma
