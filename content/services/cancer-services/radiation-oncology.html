Radiation Oncology

Schedule an Appointment

The expert team of healthcare professionals at Riverview Health Cancer Center is here to help guide you through the uncertainty and challenges that come with a cancer diagnosis. Our world-renowned physicians pair their clinical expertise with a full range of sophisticated treatment options to combat cancer and provide you with the best possible outcome.

Our center is home to some of the most advanced cancer treatments available, including intensity-modulated radiation therapy (IMRT). IMRT is an innovative therapy that allows our doctors and clinicians to deliver a targeted dose of radiation to a tumor while avoiding healthy tissue. This treatment option is one of the most effective cancer therapies in the world, and is available right here at Riverview Health.

IMRT can be used to treat many cancers, including those of the prostate, breast, head, neck, lung and pancreas. The precision of IMRT helps doctors completely eliminate tumors, even in difficult-to-reach areas that were considered too risky to treat with radiation in years past.

Through it all, our dedicated team is here to help answer your questions and provide guidance in your battle against cancer. We do this with steadfast determination, while keeping compassion for your needs at the forefront of our minds.

Conditions We Treat

There are hundreds of types of cancer, with some being much more common than others. The following are just some of the types of cancer we can diagnose and treat.

  • Brain tumors
  • Breast cancer
  • Endocrine cancer
  • Gastrointestinal cancer
  • Genitourinary cancer
  • Gynecologic cancer
  • Head and neck cancers
  • Hematologic (blood) cancer
  • Hepato-pancreato-biliary cancer
  • Prostate cancer
  • Sarcoma
  • Skin cancer and melanoma
  • Thoracic (lung) cancer
  • Thymoma
