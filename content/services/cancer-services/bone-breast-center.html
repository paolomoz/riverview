If you’re like many women, you may be taking care of your family, your career or any number of other responsibilities. And in the midst of managing all those priorities, it can be easy to let your own healthcare slip. At Riverview Health Bone & Breast Center we’re proud to be your partner in helping you stay well.

As an accredited Breast Center of Excellence, we offer a full range of services specifically designed for women—including mammograms, breast biopsies, ultrasounds and osteoporosis screenings, such as DEXA scans—all in one convenient location. With accreditations in Breast Ultrasound, Biopsy, FNA and Stereotactic Breast Biopsy, we have the latest technology on-hand to provide you with the highest level of care.

Located in the Riverview Health Women's Pavilion, our spacious center is designed to make your visit a pleasant one. Our patient care team provides private treatment and consultation in rooms decorated in rich colors that exude warm sophistication. Loved ones can wait in the comfortable lounge or in many other special areas of the Women's Pavilion, including a serene retreat and peaceful healing garden.

Services We Provide

Mammography

Screening mammography—A screening mammogram is for a woman who isn't experiencing any new problems in her breast. Looking for mammograms near me? Mammograms can help detect breast cancer early, when it’s easier to treat. Find a nearby location and book your appointment today to stay proactive and take care of yourself.

Take our questionnaire to self-schedule online

Computer-aided detection—After reviewing your mammogram, our radiologists refer to areas of interest marked by a computer, referred to as R2 Image Checker. This combination of information provides the most accurate diagnosis.

Diagnostic mammography—A diagnostic mammogram is necessary when a new symptom arises, when further evaluation is required as a result of previous imaging studies, or when there’s a personal history of breast cancer.

When mammograms are evaluated, our physicians review your previous mammogram images as well to help identify changes or instability in your breast.

Body Composition Scan

The body composition scan is a simple low-dose X-ray that allows us to evaluate muscle, fat and bone composition in the body. The initial scan serves as a baseline and follow-up scans provide a method of measurement to monitor improvement in combination with a person's wellness plan. Insurance is not billed.

Schedule online by taking our questionnaire

Breast Ultrasound

Ultrasound images of the breast are taken to complement mammography, to evaluate palpable lumps, and as imaging guidance for procedures, such as cyst aspirations and drainage of various breast fluids.

Breast Biopsies

Vacuum-assisted core biopsy with stereotactic and ultrasound guidance—This image-guided biopsy is quick, accurate, needs no recovery time and does not cause scarring of your breast.

Pre-operative needle localization with excisional biopsy—The needle localization portion of this procedure is done in the Women’s Center, and the excisional biopsy is done in the Riverview Health Surgery Center.

Various other breast procedures—Additional procedures may be offered as necessary.

Specialized Breast Care Team

Should you need a biopsy, our specialized breast care team will work with you to ensure prompt testing. If a cancer diagnosis is made, our team will work with you to develop an individualized care plan and help explain treatment options.

DEXA Scan or Bone Mass Measurement Test

A DEXA scan can help detect osteoporosis before a fracture occurs, as well as help determine an individual’s risk for fracture and monitor the effects of treatment.

Community Education

We provide health education through various programs, including women’s seminars, health fairs and community days. These efforts encourage women to seek screenings in a comfortable atmosphere for a variety of health issues.
