Whether you’re expecting a baby or are in need of specialty post-surgical products, the experienced staff of Riverview Health Women’s Boutique is here to meet your needs in a compassionate, caring and sensitive manner.

Riverview Health Women’s Boutique is an accredited Durable Medical Equipment provider and our staff will gladly work with your health insurance provider, when applicable.

At Riverview Health Women's Boutique, guests can ask the most personal of questions and receive the most professional answers. It’s a place where you can shop with dignity and privacy for products to help you look and feel your best.

Product offerings in the boutique are designed around the many health needs of women in all phases of life. We’re pleased to offer:

Post-breast surgery products

For women who have undergone breast surgery, Riverview Health Women’s Boutique offers traditional and attachable breast forms, partial forms and post-mastectomy bras. Our staff is expertly trained and certified to ensure a proper fit in a discrete and caring environment.

Hair loss products

For women who are undergoing cancer treatment, Riverview Health Women’s Boutique offers an array of wigs and partial hair prostheses, as well as hats, scarves and traditional turbans.

Maternity products

For new and expectant mothers, Riverview Health Women’s Boutique provides a full line of prenatal, breastfeeding and infant-care items. We strive to make your parenting journey as smooth and comfortable as possible by offering these items in a convenient location. Riverview Health Women’s Boutique also offers a breast pump rental program.

Compression therapy

Riverview Health Women’s Boutique has custom compression hosiery designed to help you manage lymphedema, edema, varicose veins and more.

In addition, Riverview Health Women’s Boutique offers spa products, jewelry and unique gifts to pamper yourself or another special woman.
