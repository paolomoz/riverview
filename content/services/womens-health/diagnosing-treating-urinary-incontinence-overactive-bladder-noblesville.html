Urinary incontinence can cause an individual to have trouble holding their urine. This may be due to a weak muscle around the urethra, called the sphincter. This condition is very common, and it can affect individuals differently, depending on the cause.

Overactive bladder is a similar condition that causes a person to experience a sudden urge to urinate. It’s most commonly seen in women, although some men may experience this condition.

Are you tired of these conditions affecting your quality of life? At Riverview Health, our OB/GYNs in Noblesville, IN, can help diagnose urinary incontinence and overactive bladder and refer you to a specialist through our close partnerships with urology experts.

Symptoms of Urinary Incontinence & Overactive Bladder

Common overactive bladder symptoms include:

A sudden urge to urinate

An urgent need to urinate followed by a leakage of urine

Frequent urination (more than eight times in a day)

Loss of sleep due to frequent urination

Leakage of urine if you cough, sneeze, laugh or exercise

A sudden urge to urinate

Frequent urination that affects everyday activities
