Nuclear cardiology is a branch of nuclear medicine used to diagnose and treat heart disease. Using radioactive materials, doctors use nuclear imaging tests to evaluate the structure and function of your heart’s tissues and cells. Nuclear cardiology is performed at Riverview Health.

Why Nuclear Cardiology Is Performed

At Riverview Health, nuclear medicine doctors use diagnostic testing to assess the following heart conditions:

  • Abnormal heart rhythms
  • Coronary artery disease
  • Heart defects
  • Heart failure

Nuclear Cardiology Services at Riverview Health

Common nuclear imaging tests we offer to diagnose a variety of heart problems include:

  • MUGA scans to assess the symptoms of heart failure
  • PET scans to evaluate cancer, heart disease and disorders of the nervous system
  • SPECT scans to show blood flow in the heart using 3D images and help doctors diagnose heart and blood vessel conditions
