Heart disease is a general term for problems with your heart, including:

Heart Disease Symptoms

Backache

Chest pain, arm pain or neck/jaw pain with activity

Fatigue

Feeling of anxiousness or restlessness

Indigestion

Lightheadedness

Nausea

Shortness of breath when you exert yourself

Swelling in your feet and ankles

Heart Disease Causes

Being overweight

Eating high amounts of fats and cholesterol

Having high blood pressure or diabetes

Living a sedentary lifestyle

Recreational drug use

Smoking tobacco

Diagnosing & Treating Your Heart Disease at Riverview Health

When you visit Riverview Health, your doctor will assess your health and medical history, perform a physical exam and go over your heart disease risk factors. Diagnostic tests, such as either a blood test, chest X-ray, EKG, echocardiogram, stress test or heart catheterization or CAT scan, may be recommended to help provide a clear picture of your heart condition.
