Cardiopulmonary rehabilitation, or cardiac rehab for short, is a medically supervised program that helps people with heart problems recover from procedures, improve their health and reduce their risk of further complications.

How Cardiopulmonary Rehab Can Help

Recover from a recent heart attack, heart surgery or procedure like angioplasty

Manage a current heart condition

Improve your heart health

Prevent future heart problems

Reduce your risk factors for heart disease such as high blood pressure or high cholesterol)

Cardiopulmonary Rehab Services at Riverview Health

The cardiac rehabilitation program at Riverview Health provides a wide variety of services for people with heart problems in Noblesville and the surrounding areas. When you visit one of our cardiac rehab gyms, you’ll have access to a medical evaluation, exercise programs, education about a heart-healthy lifestyle and counseling services to help you cope with emotions about your health.

What to Expect from Our Cardiac Rehabilitation Program

In most cases, your time in cardiac rehab will last about three months. But it could be shorter depending on your individual health needs and requirements. You’ll partner with a team of healthcare professionals, including:

A cardiologist
Dietitians
Exercise specialists
Mental health providers
Nurses
Physical and occupational therapists
Your primary care physician
