Vascular Services

Home Services

Riverview Health Heart & Vascular offers comprehensive vascular care available 24 hours a day, seven days a week to treat conditions of the arteries and veins.

Our board-certified vascular surgeons, interventional radiologists and interventional cardiologists are specialty trained in peripheral vascular disease using the most advanced technology. In addition to diagnostic services, we provide a full range of non-invasive and surgical procedures to repair and treat diseases of the blood vessels.

Furthermore, the experts at Riverview Health Heart & Vascular are dedicated to helping you improve your health with tools and resources designed to educate and guide you in disease prevention and early detection.

Screening and Early Detection

Vascular disease can progress quietly, with few symptoms until a blockage or aneurysm becomes serious. That is why early detection matters. Riverview Health offers a vascular screening package that checks the carotid arteries in the neck, screens for abdominal aortic aneurysm, and uses an ankle brachial index test to look for peripheral artery disease in the legs. If a screening raises a concern, our vascular team can move directly to further diagnostic testing and treatment planning without you having to start over somewhere new.

Connected Heart and Vascular Care

Because arteries and veins are part of a larger cardiovascular picture, vascular care at Riverview Health is delivered alongside our full range of heart and vascular services. You can also schedule a heart scan to assess your cardiac health, and your results are shared with your care team so treatment decisions account for your whole cardiovascular health.
