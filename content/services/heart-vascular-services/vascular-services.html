Vascular Services

Schedule an Appointment

Riverview Health Heart & Vascular offers comprehensive vascular care available 24 hours a day, seven days a week to treat conditions of the arteries and veins.

Our board-certified vascular surgeons, interventional radiologists and interventional cardiologists are specialty trained in peripheral vascular disease using the most advanced technology. In addition to diagnostic services, we provide a full range of non-invasive and surgical procedures to repair and treat diseases of the blood vessels.

Furthermore, the experts at Riverview Health Heart & Vascular are dedicated to helping you improve your health with tools and resources designed to educate and guide you in disease prevention and early detection.

Diagnostic Services

  • Lower extremity arterial doppler
  • Carotid, renal and vascular ultrasounds
  • Vascular CT angiogram
  • Vascular magnetic resonance angiography

Interventional Procedures

  • Angiograms
  • Balloon angioplasty
  • Endograft repairs for aortic aneurysms
  • Laser atherectomy
  • Thrombolytic therapy
  • Vascular stent placements

Surgery

  • Aortic aneurysm repair
  • Carotid endarterectomy
  • Extremity bypass surgery
  • Renal bypass surgery
  • Vein ligation surgery

Conditions We Treat

For urgent or non-urgent vascular care, Riverview Health Heart & Vascular is your expert resource for treatment, 24 hours a day, seven days a week. The following are just some of the vascular conditions we treat.

  • Aneurysms
  • Carotid artery disease
  • Deep vein thrombosis (DVT)
  • Peripheral artery disease
  • Pulmonary embolism
  • Renal artery disease
  • Symptoms of vascular disease
  • Thoracic outlet syndrome
  • Varicose veins
  • Venous disease
