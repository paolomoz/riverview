Athlete Body Composition Assessment

Riverview Health is proud to offer body composition scans for athletes and active individuals looking to improve their strength and conditioning. This quick, accurate scan measures a person’s lean mass, bone mineral content and body fat percentage. The data obtained from the body composition scan provides participants with valuable information to assist with personal training programs.

The overall appointment lasts 30 minutes. After participants complete their scan, a Riverview Health exercise specialist will review the results and answer questions during a consultation segment of the appointment. Body composition scan results can be shared with the client’s physician, coach or athletic trainer to help create a personalized exercise routine.

Participants are encouraged to wear comfortable clothing to their appointment (no metal zippers, buttons, belts, etc.), and to avoid taking calcium supplements 24 hours prior. Participants may eat and drink normally the day of their appointment.

Personal Training for Non-Athletes

You don’t have to be an athlete to benefit from personal training. If you are an active adult, this program is for you. Our exercise specialists are dedicated to helping you care for your body and reach your fitness goals. Together, you and your exercise specialist will work to design an exercise plan that is tailored to your specific wants and needs. This program is great for beginners who need assistance with creating an exercise regimen or for fitness buffs looking to add something new to their workouts.
