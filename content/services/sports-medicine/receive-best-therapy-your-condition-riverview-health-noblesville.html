Receive the Best Therapy for Your Condition at Riverview Health in Noblesville, IN

Schedule an Appointment

Therapy—including physical, occupational and speech—provides a way to recover from an injury, surgery or disease to improve your condition and quality of life. At Riverview Health, we offer a wide range of therapies to help people reduce pain levels and maximize their abilities.

Types of Therapy at Riverview Health

Come to Riverview Health to receive any of the following therapies.

Physical Therapy

In addition to rehabilitation after a surgery or injury, physical therapy provides exercises that help aid a full recovery. Our licensed physical therapists will create a customized plan for you, depending on your condition, and work with you to meet your goals.

Occupational Therapy

Mental, physical and cognitive disorders may keep you from completing daily tasks. Our occupational therapists teach you to accomplish basic skills needed to live a more independent life.

Speech Therapy

Stroke, Parkinson’s disease, multiple sclerosis and other neurological issues can impact your ability to speak or swallow. The speech therapists at Riverview Health work with patients to use the proper muscles to complete important activities without assistance.

Pediatric Therapy

Our pediatric therapists are friendly and have experience working with a number of pediatric disorders, including autism, Down syndrome, spina bifida, torticollis, brain injury and cerebral palsy. We offer pediatric occupational therapy, physical therapy and speech therapy.

Aquatic Therapy

Riverview Health offers aquatic therapy in our warm water pools at our Rehab & Fitness facilities in Noblesville and Carmel. This type of therapy is used for people of all ages, from infants to seniors. Our facility features a chair lift to offer easy access in and out of the pool for those who need assistance.

If you’ve recently gone through surgery, experienced a stroke or are looking to develop certain skills, reach out to our therapy schedulers at 317.776.7247.

Riverview Health offers several types of therapy for people living in Noblesville, Carmel, Cicero, Fishers, Sheridan and Westfield, IN.
