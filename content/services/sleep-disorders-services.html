Sleep Disorders Services

Schedule an Appointment

Restful, quality sleep is essential to function properly, both physically and mentally. When your sleep is disrupted, the effects can have a negative impact on not only your mood and abilities, but also your health. Heart disease, obesity and an impaired immune system are just a few problems that can result from chronic sleep issues.

Riverview Health is your trusted resource for diagnosing and treating a wide range of sleep disorders. If you have difficulty falling asleep or staying asleep, often feel tired or sleep too much, there may be a medical explanation. In most cases, sleep disorders can be easily managed once they’re properly diagnosed.

With a referral from your family physician or specialist, our expert team will perform an evaluation to see if you have a sleep or wake disorder. Our team includes board-certified sleep physicians, registered sleep technologists, sleep apnea educators and certified respiratory therapists—all dedicated to providing you with personal and effective care.

Our program is accredited by the American Academy of Sleep Medicine and offers a wide range of studies performed on an outpatient basis with your comfort and convenience in mind. We perform sleep studies during the day and night in a comfortable atmosphere as close as possible to your normal sleep time. You’ll simply visit our facility at a scheduled time, and while you sleep, our team will monitor and track your sleep patterns.

The results of your study help is determine ways to improve your sleep. We’ll work with you to find solutions that can lead to a more restful night’s sleep, and a fresh start to improving your health.

Conditions We Treat

At Riverview Health, we can diagnose and treat a variety of sleep disorders, including:

  • Advanced/delayed sleep phase disorder
  • Bruxism (teeth grinding)
  • Insomnia
  • Irregular sleep/wake syndrome
  • Narcolepsy
  • Obstructive sleep apnea
  • Restless leg syndrome
