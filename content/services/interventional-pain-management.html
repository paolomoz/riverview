Interventional Pain Management

Schedule an Appointment

Various conditions can cause chronic pain in your neck, back and hips. Interventional pain management can help diagnose and treat many painful conditions.

Riverview Health offers two interventional pain management centers, as well as a medication management program. Our interventional pain management team will spend as much time as you need to listen to your concerns, clearly evaluate and diagnose your conditions and proceed with your individualized treatment plan.

If you are suffering from pain that interferes with your daily life, ask your primary care provider for a referral.

Services include:

  • Diagnostic nerve root blocks
  • Epidural blood patch
  • Epidural steroid injections
  • Evaluation and recommendations to referring specialties
  • Intercostal nerve blocks
  • Intrathecal targeted drug delivery systems
  • Kyphoplasty
  • Intra-articular joint injections
  • Neurolytic injections for neurological disorders
  • Pain pharmacotherapy (medication management)
  • Peripheral nerve blocks
  • Radiofrequency nerve ablation
  • Sacroiliac joint injections
  • Spinal cord stimulation therapy
  • Sympathetic nerve blocks
  • Trigger point injections

Conditions We Treat

  • Arthritic pain
  • Back, hip and neck pain
  • Cancer pain
  • Cervical and lumbar radiculopathy
  • Degenerative spine disease
  • Extremity pain
  • Fibromyalgia
  • Herniated discs
  • Neuropathic pain
  • Sciatica
  • Spinal compression fracture
