Is your business struggling to provide affordable, accessible healthcare? Skyrocketing premiums, limited access, long scheduling delays, and employee frustration are common struggles. You want to offer quality care, but traditional insurance models may not always be feasible.

Riverview Health offers a solution with Direct Primary Care through our Employer Health Centers. We understand the unique challenges faced by businesses, and we've created a healthcare model that prioritizes your employees' well-being and your bottom line.

Direct Primary Care eliminates the complexities of traditional insurance-based models, offering a simple, membership-based approach. For an affordable monthly fee, your employees gain direct access to comprehensive primary care services including:

Wellness, acute and chronic care.

Preventive services and disease management.

Convenient access to lab services, telemedicine and weekend availability.

Extended benefits to families.

Personalized care, including access to dietitian and fitness services.

Boost employee health and productivity. Healthy employees are more engaged and productive. Direct Primary Care promotes proactive care, leading to improved overall well-being.

Attract and retain top talent. Offering exceptional healthcare benefits sets you apart as an employer of choice.

Simplify healthcare access by eliminating the complexities of insurance claims. Direct Primary Care provides straightforward, hassle-free access to care.

Cost-effective affordable monthly fees provide budget certainty, helping you manage healthcare expenses effectively.

We're committed to helping your business thrive by providing accessible, affordable, and high-quality healthcare. Let us partner with you to create a healthier and more productive workforce.
