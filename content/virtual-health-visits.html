Virtual Health Visits

It’s not always easy to keep your health a priority. That’s why we want to make it as easy as possible to stay on top of your health. With a virtual visit, you can see a doctor from the comfort of your home or anywhere you choose.

Please note that if you are experiencing any serious or life-threatening conditions such as chest pain, difficulty breathing or injuries that need to have an X-ray or image taken, please call 911 or go to the emergency room.

You must be a current Riverview Health patient.

You must have a MyChart account. Need an account? To request an access code, please contact your Riverview Health provider or call 317.776.7141 and press option 2. Need help troubleshooting? Please call 1.855.364.7936.

You must have a video visit appointment scheduled through your provider's office. You will need to call and set this up like you would an in-person appointment.

Download the MyChart app on your smartphone or tablet:iTunes App Store (Apple iOS) | Google Play Store (Android)

Get started on your computer by logging into your MyChart account here: https://riverview.org/mychart/
