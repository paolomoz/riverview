Telehealth Services: Convenient Care at Your Fingertips

It’s not always easy to keep your health a priority. That’s why we want to make it as easy as possible to stay on top of your health. With a virtual visit, you can see a doctor from the comfort of your home or anywhere you choose.

Please note that if you are experiencing any serious or life-threatening conditions such as chest pain, difficulty breathing or injuries that need to have an X-ray or image taken, please call 911 or go to the emergency room.

See a Riverview Health Provider Using MyChart

Please note the following for MyChart Video Visits:

  • You must be a current Riverview Health patient.
  • You must have a MyChart account. Need an account? To request an access code, please contact your Riverview Health provider or call 317.776.7141 and press option 2. Need help troubleshooting? Please call 1.855.364.7936.
  • You must have a video visit appointment scheduled through your provider's office. You will need to call and set this up like you would an in-person appointment.

Download the MyChart app on your smartphone or tablet:iTunes App Store (Apple iOS) | Google Play Store (Android)

Get started on your computer by logging into your MyChart account here: https://riverview.org/mychart/

Setting Up for a Smooth Video Visit

A little preparation goes a long way toward a glitch-free telehealth appointment:

  • Install the MyChart app on the smartphone or tablet you plan to use, or confirm you can sign in to MyChart on your computer, well before your appointment time.
  • Pick a quiet, well-lit spot with a reliable internet connection so your provider can see and hear you clearly.
  • Keep a list of your current medications and any questions for your provider within reach, just as you would for an office visit.
  • If the video connection gives you trouble, the MyChart troubleshooting line at 1.855.364.7936 can help you get reconnected.

Common Telehealth Questions

Do I have to be an established Riverview Health patient?

Yes. MyChart video visits are available only to current Riverview Health patients, and the appointment must be scheduled by calling your provider's office.

How do I get MyChart access before my first video visit?

Ask your Riverview Health provider for an access code, or call 317.776.7141 and press option 2 to request one. You can read about everything the portal offers on our MyChart overview page.

Is a video visit appropriate for every health concern?

No. Serious or life-threatening symptoms — chest pain, difficulty breathing, or injuries that may need an X-ray — require calling 911 or going to the emergency room. For everything in between, you can compare in-person options on our schedule an appointment page.
