Schedule My Mammogram

We offer compassionate, convenient mammogram appointments at our Noblesville and Westfield locations—because your health deserves priority.

We offer compassionate, convenient mammogram appointments at our Noblesville and Westfield locations—because your health deserves priority.

Mammograms are recommended annually for women starting at age 40. Regular screenings can catch breast cancer in its early stages when treatment is most successful. Don't delay; take control of your health.

Welcome to mammogram scheduling. For your convenience we offer appointments at both our Noblesville and Westfield locations. To schedule your mammogram online, there are a few qualifying questions we’ll need to ask you to make sure you qualify for direct scheduling. If you prefer to schedule your mammogram by phone, please call 317.776.7247.

Mammogram Locations

For more information about screening mammography, contact the location nearest you:

  • Westfield Hospital — 17600 Shamrock Blvd, Westfield, IN 46074-7002. Ph: (317) 214-5555

Who Qualifies for Online Mammogram Scheduling

Before you can book directly online, the questionnaire asks a few qualifying questions, including:

  • Are you an existing patient?
  • Was your last screening more than 1 year ago from today?
  • Are you experiencing any of the following symptoms: nipple discharge, lump, constant pain?
  • Have you had breast cancer in the last five years?
  • Are you pregnant?
  • Are you currently breastfeeding or have you stopped breastfeeding in the last six months?

Depending on your answers, you may be asked to call us at 317-776-7247 so we can help schedule you, or to contact your primary care provider for further guidance before scheduling your mammogram. If you need a primary care physician, you can search the Riverview Health provider directory to find one accepting new patients.

On the Day of Your Screening

Plan to arrive with a photo ID and your insurance card so registration goes quickly. If you have had mammograms performed outside Riverview Health, be prepared to tell our team where and when they were done.
