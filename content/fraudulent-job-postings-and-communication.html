Fraudulent Job Postings and Communication

Here’s how you can spot false job postings and communications to keep yourself safe:

ALL email communication from Human Resources and staff members at Riverview Health will come from email addresses that end in @riverview.org.

Riverview Health does not use smartphone apps, such as Wire, to communicate with applicants. The primary methods of communication from Riverview Health are via email (@riverview.org) or phone call.

Any position posted on third-party websites, such as Indeed.com, CareerBuilder.com, LinkedIn.com, etc., will direct the applicant to the Riverview Health Careers site at jobs.riverview.org.

If you are asked to submit personal information to an “Apply Now” link that does not lead to a job posting on riverview.jobs.net, that link is not from Riverview Health.

If you are suspicious of a job posting or person who is communicating with you about a job at Riverview Health, please email info@riverview.org with as much detail as you can provide so we can investigate or verify the activity.
