Corporate Partners

The Business Honor Roll is a collaboration of small, mid-size and large companies throughout Hamilton County and central Indiana who support Riverview Health Foundation on an annual basis. Donations made by our corporate partners help fund Riverview Health expansions, renovations, capital equipment needs, programs and new services. Corporate gifts opportunities include event sponsorships, campaign gifts and in-kind gifts.We thank these generous companies for their support of Riverview Health.

To join our Business Honor Roll, contact Riverview Health Foundation at 317.776.7317 or mwiles@riverview.org.
