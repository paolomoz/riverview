Improving Movement with Physical Therapy in Noblesville, IN

Schedule an Appointment

Physical therapy uses non-invasive methods to help you regain function after an injury or illness. This type of rehabilitation is important for strengthening muscles, regaining balance, relieving pain and many other health goals. Anyone with an injury can benefit from physical therapy, whether you’re recovering from surgery or a sport-related injury. At Riverview Health Rehab & Fitness, our team of physical therapists work as part of our orthopedic team to give you the tools you need for a full recovery.

Do I Need Physical Therapy?

If you recently had surgery or are looking to reduce pain in certain joints, muscles or bones, our physical therapists and assistants can help. By offering the full range of physical, occupational, speech, aquatic and pediatric therapy, Riverview Health helps you recover from:

  • ACL surgery
  • Arthritis
  • Foot surgery
  • Hand surgery
  • Herniated disc surgery
  • Hip replacement
  • Knee replacement
  • Rotator cuff surgery
  • Spinal fusion

Our therapists are also experienced in methods like trigger point dry needling and the Graston Technique. Talk to us about what type of physical therapy treatment is best for your condition.

Take Advantage of Physical Therapy at Riverview Health

The goal of physical therapy is to help you regain your physical function. First, we’ll assess your current condition and its effect on your ability to function, including your range of motion and strength. This will help us create a plan for your treatment, including an exercise schedule. Physical therapy can last up to several months. During your treatment, our physical therapists will continue to access your progress and help you reach your goal of a complete recovery.
