Whether you’re a new patient or an existing patient, we’ll get you in as soon as possible. Same day appointments available for routine visits, follow-ups or new concerns.

New and existing patients can request an appointment via a simple form, and our team will follow up with next steps. You can also book directly by calling the provider’s office.

Employer health center partners can schedule appointments at our Noblesville or Westfield locations for both new and existing patients. Video visits are also available.

In just a few easy steps, you can book an appointment with one of our trusted healthcare professionals.

Existing patients can book appointments with your primary care provider through MyChart account.

Can't find what you are looking for? Call central scheduling number - 317.776.7247 to speak to our scheduling representative or click below to request an appointment and a staff member will reach out to you.

It’s not always easy to keep your health a priority. That’s why we want to make it as easy as possible to stay on top of your health. With a virtual visit, you can see a doctor from the comfort of your home or anywhere you choose.

Please note that if you are experiencing any serious or life-threatening conditions such as chest pain, difficulty breathing or injuries that need to have an X-ray or image taken, please call 911 or go to the emergency room.
