Riverview At Work: Our Mission

Riverview Health recognizes that a healthy and engaged workforce is essential to the success of any organization. Our mission is to improve and preserve the health and well-being of those we serve.

Contact Us Learn More

Why choose the Riverview Health Employer Program

Riverview Health, a 5-star Achieve Well awarded by the Wellness Council of Indiana, offers many services to help employers develop and implement a customized wellness program. Wellness programs help employees engage and change short- and long-term behaviors and gain better overall health.

Customized Solutions

We tailor our services to fit the unique needs of your business and workforce.

Comprehensive Approach

From physical health to mental well-being, we cover every aspect of employee health.

Proven Results

Our services are designed to improve productivity, reduce absenteeism, and promote long-term employee retention.
