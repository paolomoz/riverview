In 1909, Dr. Samuel Harrell had the commitment and foresight to build the Harrell Hospital and Sanatorium—the beginnings of what is now Riverview Health. Riverview Health Foundation established the Dr. Samuel Harrell Society in honor of individuals whose gifts by bequests or other estate plan provisions—including wills, trusts or annuities—demonstrate their support for the future for high-quality care at Riverview Health.

We express our thanks through offering special recognition, opportunities and events for the Dr. Samuel Harrell Society members. These include:

Permanent recognition on the recognition wall displayed in the Riverview Health Atrium
