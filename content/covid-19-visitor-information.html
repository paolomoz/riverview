COVID-19 Visitor Information

Visiting hours are limited to 8 a.m. to 8 p.m. every day. Visitors should not visit if they are ill or are experiencing upper respiratory symptoms or are under isolation/quarantine for COVID-19 exposure.

COVID-19 patients who are admitted to the hospital may have one visitor at a time who meets the guidelines above. Personal protective equipment will be provided and required during the visit. The visitor must not wander the hospital and should only go to and from the patient's room. Please contact staff with questions about additional requirements and limitations regarding COVID-19 visitation guidelines, as they are subject to change.

Visitors should use the main entrance.

Maternity patients may have four designated visitors throughout their stay (and one designated visitor who may not change and can spend the night). Visitors must meet the following criteria and be approved.
