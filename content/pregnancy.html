Expecting the Unexpected

Riverview Health is here to give you peace of mind, even when you’re planning a pregnancy and preparing your body to be healthy for your little one. There are many tests, treatments and general health recommendations specific to women who are trying to conceive that can help ensure a smoother pregnancy. Contact Riverview Health Women’s Services today to schedule a wellness visit and discuss family planning with an experienced Riverview Health OB/GYN physician.

From morning sickness to pregnancy discomforts—there's a lot to think about when you're expecting. No two pregnancies or births are exactly alike. Our experts are here for you—whether it's easing your mind by answering your questions or treating pregnancy-related conditions such as preeclampsia or gallstones.

In preparation for your baby’s arrival, Riverview Health offers an online Preparation for Childbirth class for expecting mothers and partners. This class provides a thorough overview of what to expect during childbirth.

In addition, our online breastfeeding class will help you learn more about the advantages of breastfeeding and how to best meet your infant's nutritional needs.

Learn more about our maternity tours and childbirth and breastfeeding classes on our Classes & Events page.
