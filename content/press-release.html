News From the Riverview Health Newsroom

This newsroom collects official announcements from Riverview Health, the independent, community-based health system serving Hamilton County, Indiana. Releases published here cover the stories shaping care across our Noblesville and Westfield hospitals and our network of primary, specialty and emergency care locations.

Recent announcements have included physicians and providers joining Riverview Health Physicians; expanded facilities and services, from new primary care access in Westfield to the newly expanded acute inpatient rehabilitation unit; and investments in technology such as the acquisition of three da Vinci 5 surgical robots and the launch of the CORI surgical system. You'll also find recognition news — Great Place to Work certifications, community impact awards and honors for excellence in infant and maternal health — alongside leadership updates from the board of trustees and executive team.

Fundraising milestones from the Riverview Health Foundation, including its galas, golf tournaments, scholarships and the annual Women of Vision luncheon, are announced here as well. Browse the archive below for the full history of Riverview Health news.
