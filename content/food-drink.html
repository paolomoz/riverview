Food & Drink

Whether you're a patient, visitor, or staff member, our cafeteria is designed to provide nourishing, delicious, and convenient meal options to suit everyone’s needs. Our cafeteria staff is committed to providing meals that support the health and well-being of our community. We work closely with nutritionists to ensure our meals are balanced, healthy, and tailored to meet a variety of needs.

We offer a variety of freshly prepared meals each day, with a focus on nutritional balance and taste. From hearty soups and salads to hot entrees and grab-and-go snacks, there's something for everyone.

Located on the second floor in the center of the Noblesville hospital, near the east end of the Atrium. Closest entrance is #2.

Monday-Friday from 7 a.m.-2 p.m.: Full menu of hot meal options for breakfast and lunch. Closed for staff meeting between 10am-10:30am.

Monday-Friday after 2 p.m. and on weekends: A limited number of packaged meals, snacks and beverages are available.

Located on the second floor at the west end of the atrium near the outpatient lab, imaging and Carolyn's Corner gift shop. Closest entrance is #1.

The Market is a self-service convenient location that provides coffee, packaged meals, snacks and beverages. It also has items such as technology accessories and personal hygiene items.

Located on the first floor near the pharmacy, visitor's desk and visitor's entrance lobby.

The Market is open 24/7 for self-service check out. It has packaged drinks, snacks, sandwiches, and treats.

We are pleased to offer you room service dining while you are a patient. You may place your order daily from 6:30 a.m. to 7 p.m. or may schedule your order ahead of time to receive it later.

Using a room phone, dial 52468. Or, using an outside phone, dial 317.770.2468.
