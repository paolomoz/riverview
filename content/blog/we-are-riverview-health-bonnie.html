“I have been an associate in the Food and Nutrition department at Riverview Health since 2018. I previously worked at another local health system but chose Riverview Health because I live in Noblesville and I value its more family-oriented nature.

I love working in this customer-oriented system because it allows me to connect to my town and the people who live and work around me.

In my off time, I like to work with my hands in fiber arts like cross-stitching and making hats and scarves! I also love a good book.” -Bonnie DeBolle, Riverview Health Food & Nutrition
