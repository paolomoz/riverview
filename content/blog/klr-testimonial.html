At KLR Medical Certification Training School, employee well-being is more than a priority, it's a core value. As a small business dedicated to educating future healthcare professionals, KLR understands the importance of accessible, compassionate care. Yet, finding a healthcare solution that was both affordable and high-quality proved challenging, until Riverview Health introduced its Direct Primary Care (DPC) program.

"We wanted something close to home that our team could rely on, without the worry of high costs or the impersonal feel that sometimes comes with larger systems," said Katie Reigelsperger, CEO and Founder of KLR. "Riverview Health has met these needs exceptionally well."

Through the DPC agreement, KLR employees now enjoy direct access to comprehensive primary care services at Riverview Health. The program offers same-day appointments, preventive care, wellness services, and a personalized approach that has made a meaningful difference in the lives of KLR's team.

"Our employees appreciate the ease of scheduling, the time providers take with them, and the emphasis on prevention, not just treatment," Reigelsperger shared. "It’s given our team peace of mind and helped them stay proactive and productive."

The partnership also reflects a shared commitment to community and education. KLR has long collaborated with Riverview Health to provide clinical placement opportunities for students, and this healthcare agreement strengthens that relationship.

"The Direct Primary Care program has been a wonderful asset for our team, providing personalized care, convenience, and peace of mind," said Reigelsperger.

As KLR continues to grow and support the next generation of healthcare professionals, its partnership with Riverview Health stands as a model for how small businesses can prioritize employee wellness through innovative, cost-effective solutions.

To learn more about Direct Primary Care, visit riverview.org/directprimarycare.

"The Direct Primary Care program has been a wonderful asset for our team, providing personalized care, convenience, and peace of mind."

Katie Reigelsperger, CEO & Founder, KLR Medical Certification Training School
