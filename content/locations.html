Care Close to Home in Hamilton County

Riverview Health is an independent, community-based health system rooted in Hamilton County, Indiana. Its anchor is the full-service, 156-bed Riverview Health Noblesville Hospital at 395 Westfield Rd., joined by the 16-bed Riverview Health Westfield Hospital on Shamrock Boulevard in Westfield. Beyond the two hospitals, the system operates freestanding combined emergency room and urgent care facilities — including sites in Carmel and Fishers — plus 14 distinct primary, immediate and specialty care facilities across the county, from family medicine offices in Cicero, Carmel and Noblesville to specialty care practices and employer health centers in Noblesville and Westfield.

More than 350 physicians provide care in over 35 medical specialties throughout the network — explore the full range on our services overview. Use the search and filters on this page to narrow the directory by location name, address, type or services offered, and each result links to a detail page with the address, phone number and providers for that site.
