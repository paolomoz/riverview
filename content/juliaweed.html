Julia Weed, PA

Julia Weed, PA, is a board-certified physician assistant specializing in gastroenterology, where she combines complex problem-solving with meaningful patient relationships. She is passionate about helping patients of all ages better understand and manage their gastrointestinal health.

Julia provides a wide range of gastroenterology services with a focus on patient education and individualized care. She strives to build long-term relationships and ensure patients feel heard, supported, and confident in their healthcare decisions.
