Women of Vision Giving Club Grant: Riverview Women's Boutique

Each year, Riverview Health Foundation Women of Vision Giving Club members choose several grant recipients for the money they’ve raised throughout the previous year. Several Riverview Health services depend on this grant money each year to be able to continue and improve the care they provide to patients and community members.
