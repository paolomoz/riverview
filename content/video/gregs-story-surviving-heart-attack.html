Greg's Story: Surviving a Heart Attack

In September 2014, Lapel Schools Band Director Greg Scott suffered a massive heart attack and was brought by ambulance to Riverview Health. Heart disease is the number one killer of men and women, but not for Greg. Against all odds, he survived and is back teaching in the band room. Learn more about heart disease prevention by knowing your numbers and getting a heart or vascular screening before it’s too late.
