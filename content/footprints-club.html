Footprints Club

To celebrate the birth or to honor the loss of a child or grandchild and create a lasting impact, we invite you to become a member of the Footprints Club by making a one-time gift of $1,000 in honor of your child or grandchild.

Your gift will demonstrate the importance of generational philanthropy and will continue to build a legacy in our community for excellent care at Riverview Health for years to come. Your name and the name of your child or grandchild will be displayed in the Riverview Health Maternity Center waiting area.

There is no age limit for recognition of your child or grandchild. With your donation, you can specify an area of Riverview Health that you would like to support. When making a donation in honor of your child or grandchild, be sure to write "Footprints Club" in the comment box at the bottom of the form.

To join our list of members, contact Riverview Health Foundation at 317.776.7317 or mwiles@riverview.org.
