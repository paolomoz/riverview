Anne Mattingly, MD

Dr. Anne Mattingly is a board-certified Breast Surgical Oncologist with a passion for providing comprehensive, patient-centered care. She earned her medical degree and completed her residency at Indiana University School of Medicine. Following her residency, she completed a specialized fellowship at H. Lee Moffitt Cancer Center. She is certified by the American Board of Surgery.

Dr. Mattingly's philosophy of care is to place the patient at the center of their treatment journey. She and her team build a supportive network of resources, working together as a "village" to empower each patient in their fight against cancer. Dr. Mattingly finds deep fulfillment in her work, from the precision of surgery to the lifelong relationships she builds with her patients, celebrating their survivorship with them.
