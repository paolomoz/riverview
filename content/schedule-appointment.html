Which Scheduling Option Is Right for You?

Riverview Health gives you several ways to book, depending on your situation:

  • New to Riverview Health? Start with the provider directory to pick a doctor, or submit an appointment request and our staff will follow up with you.
  • Already an established patient? Booking through your MyChart account is the fastest route for primary care visits.
  • Prefer to choose by convenience? Browse Riverview Health locations across Hamilton County and pick the office closest to home or work.
  • Covered through an employer partner? Use the Employer Health Center link above to book your clinic visit.

Scheduling Questions, Answered

Which services offer same-day appointments?

Same-day visits are available Monday through Friday for Primary Care (317.565.0565) and Orthopedics (317.770.3777); location and provider may vary.

Can I handle my visit from home?

Many concerns can be addressed through a virtual health visit, letting you meet with a doctor by video instead of traveling to an office.

What should I do in an emergency?

Online scheduling is never the right path for serious or life-threatening symptoms such as chest pain or difficulty breathing — call 911 or go to the emergency room.
