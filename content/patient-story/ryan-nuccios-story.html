Ryan Nuccio's Story

June 23, 2025

Patient Story

Ryan Nuccio was admitted to the Riverview Health Acute Rehabilitation Unit, where a collaborative team of physicians, nurses and therapists worked together to develop a personalized care plan tailored to his unique needs. With a combination of focused physical and occupational therapy, medication management and emotional support, Ryan steadily began to regain his strength and mobility.

“After being in several of the local hospitals throughout the years, Riverview Health is my number one and I will return again and again to seek treatment,” said Ryan. “I wouldn’t let my family and friends go anywhere else.”
