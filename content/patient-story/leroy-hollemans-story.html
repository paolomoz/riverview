Leroy Holleman's Story

June 23, 2025

Patient Story

Rehabilitation

Leroy Holleman came to Riverview Health following a serious fall that led to a brain bleed. Facing a long road to recovery, Leroy was admitted to the Riverview Health Acute Rehabilitation Unit, where a dedicated team of therapists, nurses and physicians worked closely with him every step of the way.

Through personalized care, intensive therapy and consistent support, Leroy not only regained his strength and mobility but also made a full recovery, an outcome he attributes directly to the compassionate and skilled team.

“If it weren’t for their help, I wouldn’t be where I’m right now,” Leroy claims.
