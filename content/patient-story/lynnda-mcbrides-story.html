Lynnda McBride’s Story

November 24, 2025

Patient Story

Whether someone is a long-time resident or brand new to the community, Riverview Health is honored to care for patients as they navigate their healthcare journey. Lynnda McBride, a new resident in Carmel, shared how a chance connection with Riverview Health’s orthopedic team led to life-changing care through both hip and knee replacement surgeries.

Like many patients, Lynnda came to Riverview Health feeling nervous about the idea of a hip replacement. “I had not been a patient prior to my first visit, but was very impressed,” said Lynnda. “I would not hesitate to say Riverview Health is the place to be if you want excellent care and top-notch doctors and staff.”

Through multiple visits and open, compassionate communication with Dr. Norman Mindrebo, Sara Hannon, PA, Haley Hershey, CMA and Heather Tittle, CMA, Lynnda quickly gained confidence knowing she was in capable and caring hands. A year later, she entrusted us once again for a knee replacement. “I never walk out of Dr. Mindrebo’s office without a hug,” Lynnda shared. “He’s an exceptional surgeon who’s precise in his work and always takes the time to reassure you that you’re going to be just fine.”

Now pain-free and back to living an active life, Lynnda continues to share her positive experiences with others and couldn’t be prouder of the care and outcomes our orthopedic team provided. “Since my two surgeries, I have had no pain in either my hip or knee,” noted Lynnda. “It is very nice to be able to walk my dogs and do everything I used to do, without pain or extra effort.”

Riverview Health works hard to ensure every patient feels cared for, heard, and fully supported through every step of their path to recovery. When an injury or other illness interferes with your ability to move and be active, turn to your local experts in orthopedic care.
