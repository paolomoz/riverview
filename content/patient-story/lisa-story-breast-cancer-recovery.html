Patient Story

In this patient testimonial video, Lisa shares her positive breast care experience following a diagnosis of noninvasive breast cancer. She highlights how compassionate providers, clear communication, and expert care helped her feel supported and well-informed at every step - easing her anxiety and giving her confidence in both treatment and follow-up care.
 

Dr. Bowling and Dr. Tieman also discuss how comprehensive, personalized breast care can truly make a difference in a patient’s journey.

A Simple Screening, a Life Forever Changed - Watch Lisa's Story

About the Riverview Health Bone & Breast Center

Between family, work and everything else on the calendar, personal health checkups are often the first thing women postpone. The Riverview Health Bone & Breast Center exists to make staying on top of breast health easier: an accredited Breast Center of Excellence where mammograms, breast biopsies, breast ultrasounds and osteoporosis screenings such as DEXA scans are all handled in a single, convenient location.

The center holds accreditations in Breast Ultrasound, Biopsy, FNA and Stereotactic Breast Biopsy, pairing up-to-date technology with a care team focused on privacy and comfort. Housed inside the Riverview Health Women's Pavilion, it offers private consultation and treatment rooms, a comfortable lounge for loved ones, and quieter spaces — including a healing garden — designed to take some of the stress out of an anxious day.

For patients like Lisa whose cancer is found early through screening, that same team coordinates the next steps, connecting diagnosis with surgeons and oncology specialists so no one has to navigate the process alone. Screening mammography is what caught Lisa’s cancer before she ever felt a symptom, and it remains the most reliable way to find breast cancer at its most treatable stage. Scheduling one takes only a few minutes online or by phone.
