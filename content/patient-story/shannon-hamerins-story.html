Shannon Hamerin’s Story

October 13, 2025

Patient Story

At Riverview Health, we are consistently proud of the exceptional care provided by our dedicated physicians and staff. Shannon Hamerin recently shared her journey with breast cancer, highlighting the outstanding qualities of Dr. Heiser and his entire team.

“I’ve been cared for multiple times here at Riverview, and I’ve always had great care,” stated Shannon. She praised Dr. Heiser as a wonderful, highly knowledgeable, and caring physician. His approach is characterized by clear, honest communication, ensuring patients are fully informed without “sugar-coating anything.”  

In a powerful recommendation for preventative health, she encourages others to overcome hesitancy regarding mammograms: “You need to get those mammograms. Had I not gotten that taken care of when I was diagnosed at the age of 32, I probably would not be here.”

When asked if she would recommend Riverview Health to her family and friends, her response was immediate and unwavering: “Absolutely.”
